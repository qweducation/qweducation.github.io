2.The storm signal is an indication of the wind strength and potential damage, while the rainfall warning is an estimation of the amount of rainfall that a particular area is expected to receive. While Public Storm Signal No. 1 may indicate relatively moderate winds, the red warning for rainfall signifies a potentially hazardous level of rainfall that could cause flooding and landslides.

It would not be advisable to go to school under such conditions as it could be dangerous and pose a threat to one's safety. It is important to follow the guidance of local authorities and prioritize personal safety in such situations.
