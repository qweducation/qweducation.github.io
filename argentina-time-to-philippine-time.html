6:15 + 12:00 = 18:15pm

18:15pm - 12:00 = 6:15am

Therefore, it is 6:15am in Caracas, Venezuela which is equivalent to 18:15am in 24 - hour clock system.

4. half past 8:00pm

8:30pm + 12:00 = 20:30pm

Therefore, it is 5:30am in Washington, USA. This is equivalent to 17:30am in 24 - hour clock system.

12:45am - 5:00 = 7:45pm

Convert 7:45pm to 24 - hour clock system.

7:45pm + 12:00 = 19:45pm
