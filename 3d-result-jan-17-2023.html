23. (3d⁴)(3d⁴)(3d⁴) = what is the answer?​


Answer:

27d¹²

Step-by-step explanation:

Multiply the numerical coefficients to each other which is three

3×3×3=27

The exponents has the same base so we're going to follow the Product Rule in the Laws of Exponents

[tex]x ^{m} \times x ^{n} = {x}^{m + n} [/tex]

d⁴×d⁴×d⁴= d⁴+⁴+⁴= d¹²

The final answer is

27d¹²
