What are some popular tourist destinations in the Philippines?

The Philippines is a popular tourist destination with a wide range of attractions. Some of the most popular destinations include Boracay Island, known for its white sandy beaches and vibrant nightlife; Palawan Island, home to the Puerto Princesa Underground River and stunning limestone cliffs; Siargao Island, a surfer's paradise with world-class waves and stunning beaches; Cebu City, a bustling city with a rich history and delicious food; and the Chocolate Hills, a unique geological formation in Bohol consisting of over 1,200 hills that turn brown during the dry season. Other popular destinations include Mayon Volcano, Tubbataha Reef, Vigan City, and Batanes Island.
