Step-by-step explanation:

Non-individuals have a lower income bracket but have higher withholding rates. If the gross income for the year does not exceed P720,000, then a 10% withholding is required. If the gross income is higher than P720,000, a 15% withholding tax based on the gross income should be applied.

24. Identify the highest level of measurement (nominal,ordinal,interval,or ratio) of the following data and explain why is that your answer. 1.) The final grade’s (4.0 , 3.5 , 2.5 , 1.5 , 1.0 , and 0.0 ) of a student’s in a statistics class 2.)The annual salary of all government workers in the philippines 3.) Ages of students in a statistics class


Answer:

The final grades of students in a statistics class is at the interval level of measurement. The interval level of measurement is characterized by having equal intervals between each value, but no true zero point. In this case, the difference between each grade level is equal, but a grade of 0.0 does not represent a complete absence of grade, since it is possible to have negative grades or grades below 0.0
