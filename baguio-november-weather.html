9. During summer many people visit Baguio because of the Cold weather. What do you think makes Baguio cold


What Makes Baguio Cold

What makes Baguio cold is because of a phenomenon common to areas located at higher altitudes. Some people would even be perplexed because heat rises but instead of having a warmer temperature at mountaintops, the weather is colder than those in lower areas. However, as heat rises, the air expands and makes the pressure lower. Due to this, as air expands, it cools. Aside from that, the higher the altitude, the thinner the atmosphere. Consequently, heat content decreases because the amount of matter present in the thinner atmosphere is less since the total heat content of a system is directly proportional to the amount of matter.

Additional Insight as to What Makes Baguio Cold

In addition, what makes Baguio cold is because less radiation from solar energy gets trapped at higher altitudes. Radiation or heat from the sun gets trapped under 1 kilometer from the Earth's surface. Therefore, areas near sea levels can feel the heat more than those above 1 kilometer from the Earth.
