30. Carlo climbed coconut tree. He climbed 1/3 of the tree and slipped back ¼ of the total height. He then climbed 5/12 of the tree again. What part of the tree is Carlo located now?


Answer:

1/2 of the tree

Step-by-step explanation:

Transform this word problem into an equation.

1/3 - 1/4 + 5/12 =

Change the denominators into the same number. Which, in this case, is 12. Multiply the numerators by whatever number you multiplied to get the lower number.

1/3 times 4 will be 4/12,

1/4 times 3 will be 3/12,

and 5/12 will stay the same.

4/12 - 3/12 = 1/12

1/12 + 5/12 = 6/12(which can then be simplified into 1/2.
