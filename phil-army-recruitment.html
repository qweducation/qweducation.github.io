10. An army recruit is on a training exercise and instructed to walk due west for 8 km, then in a north-easterly direction for 4 mi, and finally due north for 15,840 ft. How far will he be from where he started?​


Answer:

He will be 10 km far away from where he started.

Explanation:

In this problem, we will use the concept of resultant displacement but we will be getting the magnitude only.

Formula to use

R = [tex]\sqrt{(\Sigma x)^2+(\Sigma y)^2}[/tex]

where

R     is the resultant distance

∑x   is the sum of all horizontal distances

∑y   is the sum of all vertical distances

Given information

d₁ = 8 km (west)

d₂ = 4 mi (northeast)

d₃ = 15,840 ft (north)

R = ?

Solving the problem

First, let us assume that all the distances are in kilometers (km). Therefore, let us convert the remaining two distances to a kilometer.

d₂ = 4 mi = 6.44 km

d₃ = 15,840 ft = 4.83 km

Now, let us solve the sum of all horizontal distances

∑x = - 8 + 6.44 cos 45°

∑x = - 8 + 4.55

∑x = - 3.45 km

Here, we have:

north-easterly has an angle in between north and east direction which is 45 degrees. west as negative because it goes in the left direction

Now, let us solve the sum of all vertical distances.

∑y = 6.44 sin 45° + 4.83 km

∑y = 4.55 km + 4.83 km

∑y = 9.38 km

Finally, we are ready to solve the resultant distance. Let us use the given formula

R = [tex]\sqrt{(\Sigma x)^2+(\Sigma y)^2}[/tex]

Then, substitute the given data

R = [tex]\sqrt{(-3.45km)^2+(9.38km)^2}[/tex]

R = 9.99 km ≅ 10 km

Therefore, the distance traveled by the army recruit from where he started is almost 10 km.

21. differentiate internal recruitment from external recruitment.​


Internal Recruitment happens when the organization would hire a person belonging within their ranks while External Recruitment happens when the organization would hire a person outside from their organization.

Internal Recruitment usually happens when the organization will promote or transfer an employee to a new position. External Recruitment usually happens when the organization will hire a new employee from another company and usually through an extensive hiring process using print media or employment fairs.
