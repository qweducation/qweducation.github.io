1. The New Clark City Food Processing Terminal and International Food Market​


ANSWER:

The New Clark City Food Processing Terminal and International Food Market is envisioned to be a “stock exchange” of fresh produce and processed products. The Project aims to attract investors from the private sector, local government units and other associations and cooperatives to invest on managing storage and other infrastructures and facilities for processing, handling, distribution of agricultural products utilizing integrated research development and technology to be set-up in New Clark City’s agro-industrial zone. The Project will consolidate food supply chain and post-harvest production system (e.g., fruits, vegetables, poultry and livestock) for Northern and Central Luzon, Metro Manila and even neighboring countries, for niche market (organic produce, halal-certified goods).

29. Jack drove her wife to mactan-cebu international airport from sm city. after 1 hour and 25 minutes, they stopped at a gas refilling station in lapu-lapu and spent another 5 minutes ther e. then it took 25 minutes to drive from there to the airport. they arrived in the airport at 2:45 p.m. at what time did they leave in sm city?


PROBLEM

The problem states that Jack and his wife drove their way from Sm city to mactan-cebu international airport. They stopped the car at a gas refilling station after 1 hour and 25 minutes, and spent another 5 minutes there. At 2:45 p.m.they arrived in the airport after leaving from Sm city.

Given:

• Jack drove 1 hour and 25 minutes

• They spent 5 minutes in the gas station

• They finally arrived at 2:45 pm (afternoon)

Solution:

Addition:

1 hr + 25 min + 5 min = 1 hr and 30 min.

They arrived around 2:45 pm, in order to get the time of their departure from Sm city, just substract 1 hr 30 min to the arrival time (2 hr and 45 min).

Subtraction:

2:45 - 1:30 = 1:15 pm (afternoon)

Answer

Jack and her wife left Sm city at exactly 1:15 pm and arrived at the mactan-cebu international airport around 2:45 pm after traveling for 1 and a half hour.
