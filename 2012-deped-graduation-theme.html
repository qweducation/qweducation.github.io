26. (2) Short Narrative 2 theme / Message - Imagery -figures of Speech -topic: Graduation Speech 500-750 words only employ these elements​


Answer:

As I stand here before you today, I can't help but feel a mix of emotions. On one hand, I am filled with pride and joy to see all of my fellow graduates here today, ready to embark on the next chapter of our lives. On the other hand, I am filled with a sense of sadness and nostalgia, knowing that our time together as students has come to an end.

As we stand here today, we are surrounded by a sea of imagery that represents our collective journey through education. The caps and gowns we wear, the diplomas we will receive, and the proud faces of our families and friends all serve as reminders of the hard work and dedication that brought us to this moment.

But beyond the physical symbols of our accomplishment, there are deeper layers of imagery that we carry with us. We each have our own personal memories and experiences that make up our unique educational journeys. For some of us, it may be the late nights spent studying for exams or the moments of self-doubt that we had to push through. For others, it may be the friendships and relationships that we formed along the way or the moments of pure inspiration and excitement that we felt in certain classes.

Regardless of what our individual experiences may have been, we all share one common thread: the willingness to push ourselves and strive for excellence. We all have a deep desire to learn, grow, and succeed, and that is what has brought us to this point today.

As we move forward into the next phase of our lives, we must hold onto this sense of determination and drive. We must continue to set high goals for ourselves and work tirelessly to achieve them. We must never settle for mediocrity, but always strive for excellence in all that we do.

But as we pursue our goals and dreams, we must also remember to be kind to ourselves and to others. We must remember that success is not just about achieving our own personal goals, but about lifting others up and making a positive impact in the world around us.

As I close out this graduation speech, I want to leave you with a final image. Imagine for a moment that you are standing on a mountaintop, looking out at a vast and beautiful landscape. You can see for miles in every direction, and the world seems to stretch out before you in all of its beauty and wonder.

This, my friends, is what our future holds. We are standing on the brink of a new and exciting journey, filled with endless possibilities and opportunities. So let us embrace this moment with open hearts and open minds, and let us step forward into the future with confidence and determination. Congratulations to the Class of 2023!
