The Philippine Electrical Code provides that only a professional engineer or associate electrical engineer can sign electrical plans. 2. Electric meters used in residential construction are not supplied and installed by the power utility company. 3. The smallest size of the wire to be used in residential construction shall be 12TW wire. 4. Wiring in residential construction shall be done with PVC pipe or local made. 5. The minimum size of electric wire for service entrance in residential construction is number 4 AWG .​


Answer and Explanation:

1. A (TRUE) - This statement is based on the Philippine Electrical Engineering Law (Republic Act No. 7920) and its implementing rules and regulations, which require that only licensed professional electrical engineers or registered associate electrical engineers can prepare, sign, and seal electrical plans and related documents.

2. B (FALSE) - Electric meters used in residential construction are typically supplied and installed by the power utility company. These meters are used to measure the amount of electricity consumed by the household or building and are owned and maintained by the power utility company.

3. B (FALSE) - The smallest size of wire to be used in residential construction depends on the electrical load and circuit requirements. Different wire sizes, types, and insulation ratings are specified in the Philippine Electrical Code to ensure safety and efficiency. The appropriate wire size should be determined by a licensed electrical engineer based on the specific electrical design and requirements.

4. A (TRUE) - The Philippine Electrical Code specifies the allowable wiring methods and materials for different applications, including residential construction. Wiring in residential areas should be done using approved wiring methods, such as PVC conduit or other approved materials that meet safety standards.

5. A (TRUE) - The minimum size of electric wire for service entrance in residential construction is number 4 AWG (American Wire Gauge), as specified in the Philippine Electrical Code. The service entrance is the point where the electrical power enters the building from the power utility company's distribution network. The wire size and other specifications for service entrance wiring should be based on the specific electrical design and service requirements.

12. in how many ways can 4doctors, 5 nurses, 5 professors and 3 engineers be seated in a round table so that those of the professionals sit together​


Answer:

In how many ways can 5 engineers and 4 doctors be seated at a round table without any restrictions?

This is a circular permutation problem, which is to say that when you have a circular seating arrangement of individuals you must establish where the beginning/end of it is. It is assumed that there are no visual cues regarding where the beginning and end of seating is, other than the seated individuals themselves.

Thus, any one of the seated persons can occupy the “anchor” seat, which then becomes the beginning/end of the circular arrangement. The outcome of such an arrangement is that 1 seat becomes fixed as the anchor seat along with its occupant and N-1 other seats can be occupied in various permuted ways in accordance with the A2A.

In response to this A2A the number of ways = 1*8! = 40,320.
