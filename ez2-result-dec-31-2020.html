Compute the cost of direct materials used during the year. 2. Compute the total manufacturing costs for the year. 3. Compute the cost of goods manufactured during the year.


Answer:

To compute the cost of direct materials used, the following steps can be followed:

Determine the raw materials inventory at the beginning of the year: P26,490

Determine the raw materials inventory at the end of the year: P24,910

Subtract the raw materials inventory at the end of the year from the raw materials inventory at the beginning of the year: P26,490 - P24,910 = P1,580

Add the raw materials purchased during the year to the difference in raw materials inventory: P1,580 + P361,920 = P363,500

Therefore, the cost of direct materials used during the year is P363,500.

To compute the total manufacturing costs for the year, we can add up all of the manufacturing costs listed in the problem:

P99,085 + P42,500 + P126,750 + P8,100 + P29,220 + P46,200 + P62,800 + P9,460 + P14,980 + P76,480 = P421,865

To compute the cost of goods manufactured during the year, we can add the cost of direct materials used to the total manufacturing costs:

P363,500 + P421,865 = P785,365

Therefore, the cost of goods manufactured during the year is P785,365.
