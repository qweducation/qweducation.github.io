23. homeowner co-opted to construct his house near the beach what are the necessary precautions to promote longevity of roofing ?explain​


Answer:

1. Make sure to use high-quality materials such as galvanized steel or aluminum that are resistant to rust and corrosion.

2. Use an underlayment material, such as felt paper, to protect the roofing material from the elements.

3. Install a ridge cap along the ridge line of the roof to protect it from water infiltration.

4. Install flashing around all penetrations and joints in the roof decking and around chimneys and skylights.

5. Apply a waterproof membrane along the edge of the roof decking and around any penetrations.

6. Use high-grade sealants to ensure all penetrations and joints are properly sealed.

7. Use a high-performance coating system to protect the roof from the sun’s UV rays.

8. Ensure all roofing materials are securely fastened.

9. Inspect the roof regularly for signs of wear and tear.

10. Have the roof professionally cleaned and inspected at least once a year.
