If 50 whole fried chicken were sold, what is the profit? Solution: Explanation:​


Answer:

The profit is P2000

Step-by-step explanation:

The income is: 250.50=P12,500

The cost is: 200.50 + 500 = P10,500

The profit = income - cost

= 10,500 - 125.00

= 2000

so the profit is P2000

Profit = income - cost
