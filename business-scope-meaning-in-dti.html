14. scope and delimitation meaning


Answer:

Scope and delimitation are elements of the research paper that set the boundaries for the study itself.

In simple terms, the scope of the study is the "what" and "who" of the study. It defines the subject of the study and the parameters wherein the study will be conducted because the researcher won't be able to cover everything in the field. It deals with the general aspect of what is to be studied

On the other hand, delimitation is the "how many", "how far", "how much", "how long" or "where". Delimitation narrows down which part of the scope will be studied. It deals with the specifics of what is to be studied.  

These two are needed to clearly define the subject and area of the research study.

18. what is the connection of dti, dole, dilg, dswd, doh, deped in business


the connections of the following agencies are as follows:
DTI- ensures that all business transactions, including material resource and business management are pursuant to trade laws and business protocol
DOLE- monitors job adequacy as well as labor protection for the business' employees so as avoid the abuse of powers as the Labor Code is concerned
DILG- implements protocols and policies to maintain proper business standards in the local government thereby promoting good governance procedures.
DSWD- ensures constant and periodic monitoring of social stability and promoting measures for a gender and age appropriate environment that will enable social stability among all its stakeholders.
DOH- constantly evaluates occupational safety and health measures being implemented by the business establishment. Including the psychosocial health of its employees and executives.
DEPED- implicates the course of business to every discussion or lesson especially on subjects and skills that may enable the learner to propagate business protocols.
