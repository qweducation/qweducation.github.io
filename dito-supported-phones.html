18. mobile phone art is a technology base art form. support this statement


Answer:

Mobile phone art is a form of digital art that is created using a smartphone or tablet device. With the advent of technology and the widespread availability of mobile devices, artists have been able to explore new forms of expression and creativity using the technology at hand.

Mobile phone art is a technology-based art form because it relies heavily on the use of technology to create, edit, and share artworks. From the use of digital cameras and touch screens to the availability of editing and sharing apps, artists have been able to create a wide variety of digital art pieces using only their mobile devices.

In addition, mobile phone art is a technology-based art form because it is often shared through digital channels, such as social media platforms and online galleries. This allows artists to reach a global audience, share their works with others, and connect with other artists from around the world.

Furthermore, the technology in mobile devices continues to evolve and improve, providing artists with new tools and opportunities to create and share their art. For example, advancements in virtual reality and augmented reality technology have opened up new avenues for artists to explore and create innovative pieces.

In conclusion, the reliance on technology and the use of mobile devices as the primary tool for creating and sharing art makes mobile phone art a technology-based art form.
