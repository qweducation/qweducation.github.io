A. Did the papers color change or stay the same?

The color of the paper on the construction has changed. But changes only occur on the part of the paper that is not protected by sunscreen or glasses. When exposed to sunlight, the UV light breaks down the chemical bonds in the color of the paper, causing the color of the paper to fade.

B. How is the side of the construction paper with sunblock lotion different from the side without sunblock lotion?

The side without sunscreen will change and become very white, even in a short time of less than an hour. However, the design of the sunscreen will still be vibrant. The sun's UV rays bleach the paper, but the sunscreen agent protects the color, allowing the designs of the sunscreen to still be visible.

C. How is the side of the construction paper with sundass different from the side without sunglass?

Just like sunscreen, the part covered by UV protector glasses will protect the paper from UV rays so that the color on the paper does not change where the other side of the paper will experience a significant discoloration.
