The Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD)  guidelines and programs are assessed by law with developing, administering, distributing, and implementing comprehensive social welfare programs. That aimed to improve and enhance the occupancy conditions and empower disadvantaged children, youth, women, seniors, people with disabilities, families in crisis or at risk, and communities in need. 5 social welfare and development agencies​ in the Philippines

Answer:

1. The Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program (4P) - is a human development program that invests in the health and education of poor families, primarily those with children aged 0–18.

2. Kalahi CIDSS – NCDDP - The Kapit-Bisig Laban sa Kahirapan Comprehensive and Integrated Delivery of Social Services – National Community-Driven Development Program (Kalahi CIDSS–NCDDP) - is the community-driven development program of the Philippine Government that implemented through the Department of Social Welfare and Development. Supplemented by the government of the Philippines.

3. Sustainable Livelihood Program - The Sustainable Livelihood Program (SLP) is a community-based capacity-building effort that seeks to improve the program participants’ socio-economic status through two tracks: Micro-enterprise Development and Employment Facilitation.

4. Supplemental Feeding Program - Provision of food in addition to the regular meals, to target children as part of the DSWD's ECCD program of the government.

5. ISWSFN ( International Social Welfare Services for Filipino Nationals) - is a program for migrant Filipinos and other overseas Filipino nationals who are in a crisis situation and in need of special protection and are encouraged to seek assistance in the Philippines Embassies in their countries of destination.
