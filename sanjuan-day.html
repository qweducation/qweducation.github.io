16. at sanjuan national high school, the temperature dropped from 5°C at 5P.M. to -9°C at 10P.M. find the differentintemperature


Answer:

-14°C

Step-by-step explanation:

To identify the difference in temperature, you simply have to subtract the starting temperature (5°C) from the final temperature (-9°C).

5°C –(–9°C) = 14°C

Since the temperature decreased, 14°C becomes –14°C

Confirmation :

5°C – 14°C = 9°C
