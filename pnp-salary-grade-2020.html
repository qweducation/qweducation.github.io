On other developments in Luzon, Police Regional Office Cordillera reported to the PNP Chief that 14 kilograms of marijuana bricks were recovered.

A Barangay Police assigned in Barangay Ambuklao informed the Bokod Municipal Police Station regarding suspicious items apparently found to be marijuana bricks sealed in boxes in a van from Tabuk City. The illegal stash is estimated to worth P1.68-million.

Police personnel responded in the area, and upon verification, discovered the contraband in a passenger van from Tabuk City that made a stopover in the area. A deeper investigation is underway to identify the owner of the illegal cargo. (PNP-PIO)

21. joan started a new job with annual salary of Php.150,000.00 in2007. if she receives a Php. 12,000.00 raise each year, how much will the annual salary be in 2020?with solution (ARITHMETIC SEQUENCE) THANK YOUUU!​


The formula of Arithmetic Sequence is
an= a1 + (n-1) d
a1 stands for the first term of sequence, n is the number of term, d is the common difference

Answer is 186,000

Solution
Substitute the given

a4 = 150,000 + (4-1) 12,000
a4 = 150,000 + (3) 12,000
a4 = 150,000 + 36,000
a4 = 186,000

Therefore, the annual salary in 2020 is 186,000.00
