3. FIRE PREVENTION AWARENESSQuestions:1.What is the purpose of Fire Prevention Month?2. What are the first three things you have to consider or do when there is fire. Explain each.3. Why is fire safety education and training is important? Give an example of a situation.​


Answer:

1.The fire prevention month campaign, according to the measure, promotes “safety consciousness among our people every day of the year as a positive preventive approach to a problem that can be solved by more caution, vigilance, sobriety, exercise of common sense and respect for the law."

2.The 3 things a fire needs are heat, fuel and oxygen. These three elements work together to help a fire start and take over. However, if any of the three elements are removed from a fire, then it will extinguish and no longer pose a threat.

3.Fires are damaging to property, cause injury or death, and can even eliminate jobs since many buildings destroyed by fire are not rebuilt. The way to prevent these dire outcomes is proper fire safety training. Learning about fire hazards is the key to preventing fire from occurring.
