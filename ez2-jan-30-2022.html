30. Can a charismatic and effective 30-year-old former mayor of a chartered city in Metro Manila legally run for President of the Republic of the Philippines in the 2022 elections? Explain briefly.​


Explanation:

Yes, a charismatic and effective 30-year-old former mayor of a chartered city in Metro Manila can legally run for President of the Republic of the Philippines in the 2022 elections as long as he or she meets the qualifications and requirements set by the Philippine Constitution and election laws.

The Philippine Constitution sets the following qualifications for a person to run for President:

1.A natural-born citizen of the Philippines

2.At least 40 years old on the day of the election

3.Able to read and write

4.A registered voter

5.A resident of the Philippines for at least 10 years before the election

Assuming that the former mayor meets all of these qualifications, he or she can legally run for President of the Republic of the Philippines in the 2022 elections.

It's worth noting that the former mayor would need to comply with other legal requirements such as filing a certificate of candidacy, complying with campaign finance rules, and observing election-related regulations and protocols during the campaign period
