1. Mr. Ocampo had 2,060,725 savings in a bank.He made four withdrawals of 237,498 each.Assuming that the savings does not earn any interest, about how much was left of his savings? did he withdraw almost half of his savings?Why or Why not?and its solution



Solution:

2,060,725 - 4(237,498) = amount left in savings account.

2,060,725 - 949,992 = 1,110,733 ⇒ amount left

Round off the total savings before withdrawal, what is left and the total amount withdrawn to the largest place value:

2,060,725 ⇒ 2,000,000  (total amount before withdrawal)

1,110,733 ⇒ 1,000,000  (total amount left, half of the total savings
                           before withdrawal)

949,992   ⇒   1,000,000  (total amount withdrawn, which is half of the
                       total savings before withdrawal)

ANSWER:  
1) 1,110,733 - left of his savings

2) Yes, he withdrew almost half of his savings.
     949,992  rounded off to highest place value is 1,000,000

3) The amount withdrawn is half of the savings.  By rounding off all the values (see above solution), the amount withdrawn (see answer item 2) is almost half of the total savings before any withdrawal took place.


  


2. annie withdraw 4/7 of her savings from the bank. she spent 3/4 of it on ablouse which cost php 360.00a. how much did she withdraw from her savings? b. what was her savings at first?


Let x - Total money of Annie
withdraw = 4x/7
blouse = 3/4 of 4x/7 (360)

a. P480

Soln:
3/4(4x/7) = 360
3x/7 = 360
x = 360(7)/3
x = 840

4/7 of P840 = P480

b. Her original money is P840.

3. annie withdraw 4/7 of her savings from the bank. she spent 3/4 of it ona blouse which cost Php360.00a. how much did she withdraw from her savings? b. what was her savings at first?


Savings: x

Amount withdrawn: 4/7 (x)

Amount spent on the blouse: 3/4(4/7)x  or  360.00

Find the total savings, x:

(3/4)(4/7) (x) = 360.00

3/7(x) = 360.00

x = 360.00(7/3)

x = Php 840.00  ⇒  total savings

Withdrawn from the savings:

(4/7)(840.00) = Php460.00

Check:

Amount spent on blouse is 3/4 of the amount withdrawn from the savings:

360 = 3/4(460)

360 = 360  (True)

ANSWERS:a.) Amount Withdrawn: Php 460.00b.) Savings at first: Php 840.00
