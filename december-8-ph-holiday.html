19. Jackie earns ₱ 30,000 a month for an 8-hour a day (excluding her 1-hour break time) and six times a week job. She worked overtime for a total of 20 hours last December. If we will disregard the holidays, then


Answer:

Her total earnings for December, including her overtime pay, will be ₱33,906.25.

Step-by-step explanation:

Jackie's basic salary is ₱30,000 per month, and she works 8 hours per day, 6 times a week. Therefore, she works 86 = 48 hours per week. In a month, she works for 4 weeks (assuming a 4-week month) so her total working hours for a month is 484 = 192 hours.

If she worked 20 hours of overtime last December, her total working hours for the month is now 192 + 20 = 212 hours.

To calculate her overtime pay, we will use the formula: (basic hourly rate x 1.25) x number of overtime hours

First, we need to determine her basic hourly rate by dividing her monthly salary by the number of working hours in a month:

₱30,000/192 = ₱156.25 per hour

Then we can calculate her overtime pay:

(₱156.25 x 1.25) x 20 = ₱3906.25

So her total earnings for December, including her overtime pay, will be ₱30,000 + ₱3,906.25 = ₱33,906.25.

30. Assignment: 8 What is pH?​


What is pH?

In chemistry, pH, means "potential of hydrogen".  This is a figure expressing the acidity or alkalinity of a solution on a logarithmic scale on which 7 is neutral, lower values are more acid and higher values more alkaline. The pH is equal to −log10 c, where c is the hydrogen ion concentration in moles per liter.

Answer:

pH is a measure of how acidic/basic water is. The range goes from 0 - 14, with 7 being neutral. pHs of less than 7 indicate acidity, whereas a pH of greater than 7 indicates a base. pH is really a measure of the relative amount of free hydrogen and hydroxyl ions in the water.
