To find the total amount of salary for Meng and Alden, we need to use the formula: Total amount of salary = No. of days labored X No. of worker X amount of salary/worker

In this case:

No. of days labored = 10 days

No. of worker = 2 (Meng and Alden)

amount of salary/worker = P200.00

So the calculation would be:

Total amount of salary = 10 days x 2 workers x P200.00/worker

Total amount of salary = 10 x 2 x 200

Total amount of salary = P4,000.00

Therefore, the total amount of salary for Meng and Alden is P4,000.00
