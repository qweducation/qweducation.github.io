The amount you will be charged if you take a 10 kilometer ride.

C = [(10 - 4) × 1.5] + 8 , where C is the total charged if you take a 10 kilometer ride

Solution:

C = [(10 - 4) × 1.5] + 8

C = (6 × 1.5) + 8
