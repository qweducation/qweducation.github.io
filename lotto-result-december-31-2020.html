Answer:

To find the Beginning Capital/Inventory of the owner on December 31, 2020, you can use the following formula:

Beginning Capital/Inventory = Capital (January 31, 2021) + Net Income - Drawing

Plugging in the given values, we get:

Beginning Capital/Inventory = P20,000 + P18,000 - P56,000

Simplifying, we get:

Beginning Capital/Inventory = P20,000 + P18,000 - P56,000 = P-18,000

So the Beginning Capital/Inventory of the owner on December 31, 2020 is P-18,000.

This means that the owner has a negative net worth of P18,000 on December 31, 2020.
