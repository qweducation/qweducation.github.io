16. harry is x years old father's age is four times the age of harry if father is 36 years old how old is harry​


Answer:

a. Asked: The age of Harry

b. Given: The specific age of the father. Father's age is four times the age of Harry.

c. Operation: Let Harry's age be x

d. Number Sentence/ Equation:

4 · x = 36

e. Solution:

[tex]\frac{(4)(x)}{4} =\frac{36}{4}[/tex]

x = 4 years old.

Therefore, the age of Harry is four (4) years old.
