11. a book store offers 14% discount on school supplies if mark bought 1,500 worth of school supplies how much discount did he get how much did he pay pa answer pls​


the discount that mark get is $210

discount = 14 x 1,500

100=0.14 x 1,500= 210

mark pay $1,290

final value = 1,500 - 210

= 1,290

#CARRYONLEARNING^ω^

Answer:

He'll get a discount of Php 210.00, and he'll pay Php 1,290.00.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:   [tex]R=14\%[/tex],   [tex]B=1,500[/tex]

Find:   [tex]P=?[/tex],   The amount he'll going to pay=?(Let p be the amount he'll going to pay)

Formula:   [tex]P=B{\times}R[/tex],   [tex]p=B-P[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]P=B{\times}R\\P=1,500\times14\%\\P=1,500\times0.14\\\boxed{\boldsymbol{P=210}}[/tex]

[tex]p=B-P\\p=1,500-210\\\boxed{\boldsymbol{p=1,290}}[/tex]
