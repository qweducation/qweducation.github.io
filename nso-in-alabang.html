22. Show your solution. 1. Las Piñas Engineering office installed electric posts along the stretch of Alabang-Zapote Road. They used 41 posts which were equally apart. How far apart is each post if the distance from Alabang to Zapote is 16.4 km? r, Michaela​


DIVISION: REAL-LIFE PROBLEM

Problem:

Las Piñas Engineering office installed electric posts along the stretch of Alabang-Zapote Road. They used 41 posts which were equally apart. How far apart is each post if the distance from Alabang to Zapote is 16.4 km?

Answer with Step-by-step explanation:

The required data for this word problem is the distance of two posts from each other. The given information are 16.4-kilometer distance from Alabang to Zapote and there were 41 electrical posts to be installed. The operation that we will use here is division.

By dividing 16.4 by 41, we will get a quotient of 0.4 km.

If 0.4 km will be converted to meters, just remember that 1 km is equivalent to 1,000 meters. Multiply 1,000 to 0.4, the result will become 400 meters

Therefore, each post is 0.4 km or 400 m away to the next post.

26. Why does the NSO need to survey the number of residents and population of each barangay?


In the study of Social Science, Pumapasok ang ganyang usapin sa "Demography", Demography means study of changes over a period of time in human population.

Example nito ay Birth and Death

Dito, pumapasok ang usaping Survey na yan, para malaman ng gobyerno kung ilan ang bawat meyembro sa isang pamilya. Dito rin na dedetermine yung mga sakit at ilang taon na ang bawat isa.

Halimbawa nito, sa isang baranggay na kalat ang dengue, makikita sa survey ng NSO, kung ilan at kung anong edad ang tinatamaan ng ganung sakit. Kaya nakakagawa ng Hazards o proteksiyon upang maiwasan ang isang problema sa lipunan.

Mahalaga ito, dahil ito ang pinagbabatayan ng gobyerno, maipapasok rin ang tungkol sa economics na pag didistribute ng kakailanganin ng isang buong mamamayan. Dito mapapasok ang bilang ng serbisyo at deman na kailangan sa isang baranggay.
