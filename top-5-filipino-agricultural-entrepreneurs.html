Agriculture Sector in Cagayan Valley

Among the provinces in Cagayan Valley, Isabela shared the highest number of farms with 129.7 thousand, covering 240.6 thousand hectares of agricultural land. In fact, the total farms for the province accounted for 40.3 percent of the total farms in the region.

Palay was the major temporary crop in the region in terms of area planted. It accounted for 197.3 thousand farms with a combined area of 420.5 thousand hectares.
