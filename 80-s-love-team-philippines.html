Answer:

200

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the starting score of both teams. Then,

a)

0.4x = 80 (40% = 0.4 in decimal)

x = 80/0.4 = 200

let y be the percentage of increase of B. then,

b)

y = 240/200 * 100%

= 120%
