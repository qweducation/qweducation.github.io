Advantages of Using the Median

When setting compensation, it is generally better to use the median as opposed to the mean for a simple reason: the mean or average is very sensitive to outliers (abnormally low or high values), while the median is much less affected by outliers.

Standard Deviation of Sampling Distribution of Sample Mean = Population Standard Deviation / sqrt(sample size)

28. The average monthly salary among a firm’s twelve employees is 3500 €. There are two part-time workers in the firm who both earn 2500 €/month. If the salaries of these two part-time workers are excluded from the average, what will the new average salary be?


THE AVERAGE

The new average salary is 3700

FURTHER EXPLANATION

The average is also known as the arithmetic mean, and it is defined as the sum of all numbers in a collection divided by the number of numbers in the collection. In other words, the average is the ratio of the total number of observations to the sum of all given observations.

As a result, the average formula is:

[tex]\boxed{\text{Average} =\frac{\text{ The sum of all values}}{ \text{The number of values} }}[/tex]

Example:

Find the average of 22, 13, 14, 15, 17, 25, 30

Solution

The sum of all values: 22 + 13 + 14 + 15 + 17 + 25 + 30 = 136The number of values: 7

Then, the average is

Average = 136/7 = 19.43

GIVEN QUESTION

Be Discovered

[tex]\bar{X}[/tex] = average monthly salary among a firm’s twelve employees[tex]\bar{X}[/tex] = 3500 €

monthly salary of part-time workers = 2500 €

Question:

the new average salary if the salaries of these two part-time workers are excluded from the average

Solution:

We know the average is the sum of all monthly salaries among a firm's twelve employees divided by the total of employees.

[tex]\bar{X} =\frac{\text{ The sum of all values}}{ \text{the number of values} }[/tex][tex]3500 =\frac{\text{ The sum of all values}}{ \text{12} }[/tex]the sum of values = 3500 × 12 = 42000

Then, we get the sum of all monthly salaries among a firm's twelve employees is 42000

Calculate the amount of salary for two part-time workers

total salary of two part-time workers = 2 ×2500 = 5000

The new average excludes the salary of two part-time workers, next, we will calculate the sum of the monthly salary of employees without part-time workers.

the sum of the monthly salary of employees without part-time workers = the sum of all monthly salaries among a firm's twelve employees - the sum salary of two part-time workersthe sum of the monthly salary of employees without part-time workers = 42000 - 5000 = 37000

New Average:

[tex]\bar{X}_{new} =\frac{37000}{ 10 }[/tex][tex]\bar{X}_{new} =3700[/tex]

Hence, the new average salary is 3700.
