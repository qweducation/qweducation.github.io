Korea is currently famous for its modern pop culture. Korea is actually known worldwide for this. However, even before the rise of modern Korean music and TV series, another forms of music expressed the soul of its nation. An example of this is their folk music. Folk music represents and expresses the soul and sound of Korea's traditional villages. All of these express the soul of the nation whose history is filled with various tales.

9. korea and its national health initiatives​


Answer:

The Republic of Korea is one of the few countries that has transformed itself from an aid recipient to an aid donor. It is in a unique position to share its development experience with others. Health outcomes have improved due to rapid economic development and the achievement of universal health coverage through the National Health Insurance programme.

The Republic of Korea's political commitment to Universal Health Coverage continues to be reaffirmed through hosting important World Health Assembly events including the Global Movement towards Universal Health Coverage and Access to medicines, vaccines and health products.

Explanation:

Partnering for a healthier humanity

The Republic of Korea and WHO work together to promote better health outcomes in the Western Pacific Region and globally in line with the Sustainable Development Goals. The Republic of Korea, as an important global health partner, contributes to achieving WHO priorities at all levels including surveillance and research for antimicrobial resistance, supporting WHO in health emergencies, health areas affecting an aging population like noncommunicable diseases and rehabilitation and reducing rates of disease, such as tuberculosis and hepatitis.

During the 71st World Health Assembly, the delegate from the Republic of Korea made a Statement commenting that "The Republic of Korea will remain committed to collaborating with the WHO and member states in promoting the health and well-being of world citizens." They demonstrated this by collaborating with WHO to ensure a healthy and safe Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games in PyeongChang in 2018.
