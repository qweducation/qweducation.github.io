23. What are the rank based activities most teachers often do as curricularists? Is it important? Justify


Answer:

The teacher as a curricularist implements the curriculum. It is in this role that the teacher gives life to the curriculum plan. Implementation of the curriculum by the teacher will define the success of the recommended, well-written and planned curriculum.

A person who is involved in curriculum knowing, writing, planning, implementing, evaluating, innovating, and initiating may be designated as curricularist. A TEACHER'S role is broader and inclusive of other functions and so a teacher is a curricularist.
