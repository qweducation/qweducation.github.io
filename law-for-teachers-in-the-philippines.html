Forms of the Verb

Basically, there are three forms of the verb. These are past, present, and future. Past form of the verb denotes that the action was done. Present form of the verb denotes that the action is currently happening. Future form of the verb denotes that the action will be done soon. The root form of a verb is the base form of the word.
