27. dead stars by paz marquez benitez


DEAD STARS BY PAZ MARQUEZ BENITEZ

Dead Stars is a short love story that Paz Marquez Benitez wrote in the year 1925. This short story is known as the first Filipino modern English-language short story. Dead Stars is one of Marquez's popular works.

WHAT IS DEAD STARS ALL ABOUT?

Dead Stars is about a bachelor in his 30s named Alfredo Salazar who is engaged with a woman named Esperanza but got attracted to a woman named Julia. Because of this, Alfredo lied to both of them. He didn't tell Julia that he's already engaged and he started keeping secrets from his fiance. Alfredo's wrong decision led him to bitter consequences.
