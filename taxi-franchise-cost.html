8. the cost of a taxi fare (c) varies directly as the distance (d) travelled.when the distance is 60 km,the cost is ₱35.Find the cost when the distance is 95 km​


Answer:

The cost of the taxi fare is ₱55 5/12 or ₱56 when rounded off.

Step-by-step explanation:

Equation:

[tex]c = kd[/tex]

Solution:

Solve for the constant of variation (k):

[tex]35 = k(60) \\ 35 = 60k[/tex]

Divide both sides by 60

[tex]k = \frac{7}{12} [/tex]

Solve for the cost when the distance is 95km:

[tex]c = \frac{7}{12} (95) \\ c = \frac{665}{12} \\ c = 55 \frac{5}{12} [/tex]
