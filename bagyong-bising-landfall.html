8. What is a landfall of a tropical cyclone


Landfall is when a tropical cyclone or waterspout moves from being over water to being over land. ... The effects of landfall are: the storm surge peaks. the core of strong winds comes on shore. and heavy flooding rains move over land.

Simply put, the movement of a cyclone from water to land is referred to as cyclone landfall. ... The US National Hurricane Center defines landfall as “the intersection of the surface centre of a tropical cyclone with a coastline.” Simply put, the movement of a cyclone from water to land is referred to as cyclone landfall
