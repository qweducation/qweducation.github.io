26. 3. Jireh had 72 mangoes left after selling 35 of them. He distributed the remaining mangoes equally to his 6 friends. How many mangoes did each of his friends receive?​


Answer:

12

Step-by-step explanation:

72÷6=12

sana makatolong po

To solve the problem, we need to first find the total number of mangoes that Jireh had before he sold 35 of them. We can do this by adding the 35 mangoes he sold to the 72 mangoes he had left, which gives:

72 + 35 = 107

Therefore, Jireh originally had 107 mangoes. He then distributed the remaining mangoes equally to his 6 friends. To find out how many mangoes each friend received, we can divide the total number of mangoes by the number of friends:

107 ÷ 6 = 17.83 (rounded to two decimal places)

Since we cannot distribute a fraction of a mango, we need to round the answer to the nearest whole number. In this case, we can round up to 18. Therefore, each of Jireh's 6 friends received 18 mangoes.
