18. you won the jackpot prize in a lotto draw .You with your parent decided to deposit 1,000,000.00 in a bank that offer 2% annual simple interest .After 3years , determine the interest earned.


Answer:

Php 60,000.00

Solution:

P = Php 1,000,000.00

r = 2% or 0.02

t = 3 years

Find simple interest:

(Is)Simple interest = Prt (Principal amount × interest rate× time)

Is = Prt

= (1,000,000.00)(0.02)(3)

= (20,000)(3)

Is = Php 60,000.00
