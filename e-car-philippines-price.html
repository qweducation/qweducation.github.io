5% -> 0.05 x 5 = .25

Multiply 2,000,000 to .25.

2,000,000 x .25 = 500000

Subtract 500000 to 2,000,000

2,000,000 - 500000 = 1,500,000

7. a new car is price at P200 000,and its value depreciates by 5% at the of each year.what is the price of the car after 5 years?​


Answer:

P154,756.19

Step-by-step explanation:

There's a long and short method. The long method is to manually calculate each year till you get to the year needed.

Short method:

Pd = Price depreciated in t number of years

t = no. of years

V = current value

D = depreciation rate in decimal form. {divide percent by 100 to get decimal form}

formula:

Pd = V * (1 - D)^t

Pd = P200,000 * (1-0.05)^5

Pd = P154,756.1875 or round off P156,756.19

How would the shape of the distribution change if the salesman decides to also deal in cars priced under $5,000 and in cars priced from $45,000 to $50,000 and projects sales of 200 cars in each category?


Answer:

In the given histogram, the shape of the histogram shows that the shape of the distribution exhibits symmetry (i.e. the shorter bars are to the left and to the right while the longer bars are in the middle). Adding the sales of cars priced under $5,000 and cars priced $45,000 to $50,000 with projected sales of 200 cars for each category will result in adding bars of the same size as the shortest bar to both ends of the histogram. This will not affect the initial shape of the distribution in the histogram as the distribution will still exhibit symmetry. Therefore, the correct answer to the question is "the distribution will exhibit symmetry" (option a).
