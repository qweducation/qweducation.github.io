27. did plastic envelope absorb heat?​


Answer: Yes

Explanation:Plastics are excellent insulators, meaning they can efficiently trap heat

Plastic envelopes can absorb heat to some extent depending on the type of plastic they are made of and the environmental conditions. Some plastics, such as polyethylene and polypropylene, are known to be good insulators and have low thermal conductivity, which means they are less likely to absorb heat. However, other plastics, such as polycarbonate and polystyrene, have higher thermal conductivity and may absorb heat more readily.

Additionally, the absorption of heat by a plastic envelope can be influenced by the environmental conditions. For example, if the plastic envelope is exposed to direct sunlight or placed in a warm environment, it may absorb more heat than if it is in a cooler environment.

Overall, while plastic envelopes can absorb heat to some extent, the extent of absorption can depend on the specific plastic material and the environmental conditions.
