29. Sandra has expanded her Mango float business online. She now has an online platform that accepts and processes orders. For this, she needs to hire delivery personnel to deliver the online orders. She plans to present two salary schemes for the delivery personnel. A. Weekly pay of P2,000 (Monday to Sunday) and P30.00 commission for every delivery. B. Weekly pay of P2,500 (Monday to Sunday) and P10.00 commission for every delivery. 1.If x is the number of deliveries in a week, write an equation for the total weekly salary of delivery personnel for A and B. 2.Make a table showing the values of x greater than or equal to 10 but less than or equal to 50. 3.If you are one of the applicants, and you were told that there are at most 40 deliveries in a week, which salary scheme would you choose?


1. Equation for the total weekly salary of delivery personnel:

A. Total weekly salary for scheme A = 2000 + (30 * x)

B. Total weekly salary for scheme B = 2500 + (10 * x)

Where x is the number of deliveries in a week.

2. Table for x values between 10 and 50:

x Scheme A Scheme B

10 2300 2600

20 2600 2700

30 2900 2800

40 3200 2900

50 3500 3000

3. To determine which salary scheme to choose if there are at most 40 deliveries in a week, we can evaluate the total weekly salary for each scheme when x = 40.

A. Total weekly salary for scheme A = 2000 + (30 * 40) = 3200

B. Total weekly salary for scheme B = 2500 + (10 * 40) = 2900

Based on the calculations, Scheme A would be more advantageous for the delivery personnel if there are at most 40 deliveries in a week.
