What range of scores indicates the inclusion in the retake of the exam?(Answer should be round off to whole number.)​


Answer: To solve the problem, we need to find the z-score that corresponds to the 15th percentile, which is -1.04 (using a standard normal distribution table).

Then, we can use the z-score formula to find the corresponding raw score:

z = (X - μ) / σ

-1.04 = (X - 220) / 10

-10.4 = X - 220

X = 209.6

Rounding this down to the nearest whole number, we get 209.

Therefore, the range of scores that indicates inclusion in the retake of the exam is from 0 to 209.
