6. reflection about lola basyang


REFLECTION ABOUT LOLA BASYANGWho is Lola Basyang?She is a well-known character who wrote diferent stories for children. Lola Basyang is a pen name of Severino Reyes who published stories in a Philippine magazine called, "LIWAYWAY". It became a big hit and later those stories were published in books, TV and even comics.

Lola Basyang symbolizes a grandmother. In Filipino families grandparents have a strong influence among their grandchildren. We give much respect to them and they are usually the spoiler. Our Lolos  and lolas are the ones who tell stories of the past and the lessons learned. Lola Basyang reminds us of the character influence we draw from our grandparents. Most of her stories are somehow fictional but because she is a lola, children would believe her and listen to every story while earning the lesson from it.

She is one of the characters that is already part of being a Filipino child. She is forever cherished by kids and the kids at the heart.
