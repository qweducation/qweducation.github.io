11. Note Taking (Critical Thinking, Creativity, Character) As you learn more about postmodern political ideologies, let us enhance your knowledge in applying the ideologies in real life situations. Directions: Read and analyze each question. Write your answers on a separate sheet of paper. 1. North Korean President Kim Jon-un ordered killing the first people who got infected by COVID-19 in North Korea his drastic action depicts and ideology of Totalitarianism. Explain. 2. Prime Minister Jacinda Ardern of New Zealand has been praised for her leadership style during the pandemic countries led by women seem to be particularly successful in fighting the coronavirus. How do you see world female leaders handle the pandemic more efficient than male leaders? Explain and relate to feminism. 3. Philippines among other nation suffers the effect of COVID19 Pandemic those in the low- and middle-income earners were greatly affected, how do Filipino cope with this pandemic to show our nationalism? Explain and cite an example.​


Answer:

Explanation:

The ideology of totalitarianism is characterized by a single party rule, state control of the economy, and the use of force and propaganda to maintain power. Kim Jon-un's order to kill the first people who got infected with COVID-19 in North Korea exemplifies this ideology because it shows a willingness to use extreme and violent measures to maintain control over the population, even at the cost of human lives.

Women leaders have been successful in handling the pandemic because they tend to prioritize the well-being of their citizens over political considerations. They are more collaborative and empathetic, and they are often better at communicating with the public. These qualities are often associated with feminism, which emphasizes the importance of social justice and equality. Women leaders also tend to be more open to different perspectives and less hierarchical in their decision-making, which can lead to more effective policies and greater trust in government.

Filipinos have coped with the pandemic by showing resilience and solidarity. Many have volunteered to help those in need, such as by distributing food or providing medical care. The community spirit that has emerged during the pandemic reflects a strong sense of nationalism, where people are willing to help each other and work together to overcome challenges. For example, the Bayanihan E-Konsulta program allows people to consult with doctors online for free, helping to provide medical assistance to those who cannot afford it. This program shows how Filipinos are using technology and innovation to respond to the challenges of the pandemic while demonstrating their patriotism and commitment to the common good.
