16. Choose the letter of the sentence that shows the correct sentence construction. Select one: a. Hughes Electronics can beam cable directlywith its direct TV to your home . b. Hughes Electronics can beam cable with its direct TV directly to your home . c. Hughes Electronics can beam cable directly to your home withits direct TV. d. Hughes Electronics with its direct TV can beam cable directly to your home .


Answer:

C. Hughes Electronic can beam cable directly to your home with its direct TV.

The sentence construction of the statement has the pattern of S-TV-IO-DO (subject-transitive verb-indirect object-direct object).

The subject is the main character of the statement and it is “Hughes Electronic”. The predicate is the action that the subject can do and it is “can beam cable”. It is classified as a transitive verb because the action has a receiver, which is the direct object. The indirect object is the one that determines to whom the action or the verb is done or referring to. The direct object answers the question what or whom. In the statement, the direct object is “with its TV” and the indirect object is “directly to your home”.

What can Hughes Electronic do? – “can beam cable” – To where? – “directly to your home” – Through what? – “with its direct TV”
