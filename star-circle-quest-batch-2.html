10. Dimple is making 2 ½ batches of banana cake to her school’s annual bake sale. If one batch of banana cake requires 1 ¼ cups of flour, how many cups of flour does Dimple need to make 2 ½ batches of banana cake? _____________________________?


Answer:

25/8 or 3 ¹/₈ cups

Step-by-step explanation:

This problem is a matter of ratio and proportion, in which two ratios are equal. We can express them in fractions,

1 ¹/₄ : 1  :: x : 2 ¹/₂

5/4 : 1 :: x : 5/2

(5/4) / 1 = x / (5/2)

(5/4) * (5/2) = x

25/8 = x

Therefore, we need 25/8 or 3 ¹/₈ cups of flour.
