9. Mr. Lenin sold a chair at a loss of 15% If he had sold at a markup rate of 10%, he would havegot $100 more. What is the cost price of the chair?Please with solution.​


Answer:

$400

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the cost price of the chair.

S.P (-15%) = 85% of x

S.P (-15%) = 0.85x -----(1)

S.P (+10%) = 110% of x

S.P (+10%) = 1.1x -----(2)

In (2), he got $100 more than (1).

Then, we have

(2) - (1) = 100

1.1x - 0.85x = 100

0.25x = 100

25x = 10000

x = 400

So, the cost price of the chair is $400.

15. 1. The cost price of an antique chair is P3,000.00. What is the selling price and mark-up if the sellerwants a 15% markup based on cost?​


Answer:

The selling price is P3,450 and the mark-up based on cost is P450.

Step-by-step explanation:

To get the value of the mark-up, multiply the cost to 15%.

3,000 x 0.15 = 450

To get the selling price, add the mark-up to the cost price.

3,000 + 450 = 3,450

Therefore, the selling price is P3,450 and the mark-up based on cost is P450.
