6. The cost(C )of labor varies jointly as the number of workers(w) and the number of the days(d) they work. If 8 men working 5 days each are paid P25,200.00, in how many days will it take 10 men to earn P44,100.00?(with solution)​


Answer:

7days

Step-by-step explanation:

P25,200 ÷ 8 = P3150

1 man can earn P3150 in 5 days

P3150 ÷ 5 = P630

1 man can earn P630 in 1 day

P630 × 10 man= P6,300

10 men can earn P6,300 in 1 day

P44,100 ÷ P6,300= 7

10 men can earn P44,100 in 7days

15. 45. In which of the following was the operator propositions conditional used? A. I will sleep all day or today is a holiday. B. I will sleep all day if and only if today is a holiday. C. Today is a holiday and I will sleep all day. D. If today is a holiday, then I will sleep the whole day.​


The operator proposition conditional was used in option "D. If today is a holiday, then I will sleep the whole day."

In this option, the use of the phrase "if today is a holiday, then" indicates that a conditional relationship is being established between two events: "today is a holiday" and "I will sleep all day". This is the definition of a conditional proposition, where one statement (the condition) is related to another statement (the result) using the words "if" and "then".
