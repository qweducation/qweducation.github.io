9. The Mannerism Period happened during which time period?

A. 1520 - 1580

B. 1401 - 1495

C. 1490's

D. 1520 - 1580

Answer: Mannerism Period happened around early 1520's until the beginning of the 1590's (some says in 1580). It happened between the periods of High Renaissance and the Baroque style. It was during this time that the popular art style was more on using irrational subjects and stuffs like artificial colors and poses, unclear subjects, and strives for more artistry and elegance. So, the answer is either A or D.
