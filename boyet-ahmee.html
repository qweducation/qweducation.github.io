3. 3.The sumof the ages of Roy, Boyet and Bong is 12. Boyet is 3 years younger than Roy and Roy is 6years older than Bong. How old is Boyet?​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in solving linear equation.

The problem is an application of linear equation having three unknown variables.

Let

x is age of Roy

y is age of Boyet

z is age of Bong

Solution

Create three equation to solve three unknown variables.

First Equation

The sum of The sumof the ages of Roy, Boyet and Bong is 12.

x + y + z = 12

Second Equation

Boyet is 3 years younger than Roy.

y = x - 3

Third Equation

Roy is 6 years older than Bong.

x = z + 6

Solution

x + y + z = 12

y = x - 3

x = z + 6

y = x - 3 → y = (z + 6) - 3

x + y + z = 12 → (z+6) + [(z+6)-3] + z = 12

Simplify

(z+6) + [(z+6)-3] + z = 12

z + 6 + z + 3 + z = 12

3z + 9 = 12

Solve for z

3z + 9 = 12

3z = 12-9

3z = 3

z = 1

z = 1

x = z+6

x = 1+6

x = 7

y = x-3

y = 7-3

y = 4

Check:

x+y+z= 12

7+4+1 = 12 ✓

Answer: 4 years old

23. Christian can do a job in 6 hours,if he requests Boyet to work withhim, the two can do the in 4 and 1/2 hoursHow long will it take Boyet to do the job alone?​


Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1:

A problem involving work can be solved using the formula

[tex] \frac{t}{a} + \frac{t}{b} = 1[/tex]

where T = time working together, A = the

time for person A working alone, and B = the time for person B working alone.

[tex] \frac{4.5}{6} + \frac{4.5}{ b} = 1[/tex]

Step 2:

Solve the equation created in the first step. This can be done by first multiplying the entire problem by the common denominator and then solving the resulting equation. In this case, the least common denominator is 6b.

[tex]6b( \frac{4.5}{6} + \frac{4.5}{b } ) = 1 \\ \\ 4.5b + 27 = 6b \\ \\ 27 = 6b + 4.5b \\ \\ 27 = 10.5b \\ \\ \frac{27}{10.5} = \frac{10.5b}{10.5} \\ \\ 2.6 = b[/tex]

Boyet can do it alone within 2.6 hours
