17. 1. A caretaker's monthly salary increases by Php250.00 every six month. If Lovelle receives Php4,000.00 in January 2022. How much will be her salary 5 years later? (Use arithmetic solution)​


Answer:

Php 6250.00

Step-by-step explanation:

1st: find how many terms

n= 5years/6months = 60months/6months = 10

2nd: apply arithmetic formula

An= A1+(n-1)d

= 4000+(10-1)(250)

= 4000+(9)(250)

= 4000+ 2250

= 6250

26. what are the duties of barangay kagawad? What accomplishment/achievement and specialization of barangay kagawad?​


Answer:

Under the law, the Punong Barangay shall: enforce all laws and ordinances which are applicable within the barangay, negotiate, enter into, and sign contracts for and in behalf of the barangay; maintain public order in the barangay; call and preside over the sessions of the sangguniang barangay and the barangay assembly.    They perform many official government duties, and execute minor judicial powers as part of Barangay Justice System, such as settling disputes between neighbors. Viewed as village elders, they also work informally with a number of organizations.
