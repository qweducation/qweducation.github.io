Answer:

80 passengers

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x & y be the no. of passengers from Manila to Cebu and Manila to Davao respectively.

Eq. 1: 1800x + 3500y = 494000

Eq. 2: x + y = 180

We need to determine the number of passengers that got off the plane in Cebu (x).

By substitution,

1800x + 3500(180 - x) = 494000

1800x + 630000 - 3500x = 494000

136000 = 1700x

=> x = 80, it follows that y = 100.

Thus, 80 passengers got off the plane in Cebu.

14. an airline flies from manila to davao with a stopover in cebu.it charges a fare of 1,800 pesos manila to cebu and a fare of 3,500 pesosfrom manila to davao.in one flight a total of 180 passengers boarded the plane in manila and the collected fare totalled 494,000 pesos. how many passengers got off the plane in cebu?​


Answer:

80 passengers got off the plane in Cebu.

Step-by-step explanation:

Passengers:

To Cebu: x

To Davao: y

Total passengers: 180

Equation (1):

x + y = 180

y = 180 - x  (passengers to Davao in terms of x)

Total fare:

Manila to Cebu: 1,800x

Manila to Davao: 3,500y

Total passenger fare for both destinations:  494,000

Equation (2):

1,800x + 3,500y = 494,000

Substitute 180-x to y in Equation (2):

1,800x + 3,500y = 494,000

1,800x + 3,500(180-x) = 494,000

Find the number of passengers to Cebu, x:

1,800x -3,500x + 630,000 = 494,000

-1700x = 494,000 - 630,000

-1700x/-1,700 = -136,000/-1700

x = 80 passengers to Cebu

Number of passengers to Davao, y:

y = 180-x

y = 180 - 80

y = 100 passengers to Davao

Total passenger fare for each destination:

Manila to Cebu:

1,800x = 1,800(80) = 144,000 pesos

Manila to Davao:

3,500y = 3,500(100) = 350,000 pesos

Check:

Total passengers:

80 + 100 = 180

180 = 180  (True)

Total fare:

144,000 + 350,000 = 494,000

494,000 = 494,000 (True)

Vicinal location refers to the immediate surroundings of a place. Manila is located in a bay area and surrounded by several smaller islands. Cebu is located on the eastern coast of the island of Cebu and is surrounded by smaller islands as well. Davao is located in a gulf area and surrounded by several smaller islands too.
