8. compare the time of sunrise on june 20 2020 with that on December 20 2020 on which day did the sunrise earlier​


Answer:

The sun rise earlier in June 22, 2011 (in the Philippines).  

Explanation:  

Equinox

The word equinox came from the Latin words "equi" meaning equal and "nox" meaning night.   Equinox happens when the amount of day-time is equal to the amount of night time and it is observed twice a year.  Equinox occur every time the sun's position is right above the equator.  

Solstice

There are two types of solstice, the summer and the winter solstice. The summer occurs around the month of June while the winter solstice occurs around December.

During summer solstice, longer day light or day time is experienced. On the other hand, during winter solstice,  longer night time is experienced.

Now for considering the situation of the question, according to the data, here are the time of sunrise and sunset in the Philippines for the following dates:

June 22, 2011 (Wednesday)

Sunrise: 5:28 AM

Sunset: 6:28 PM

December 22, 2011 (Thursday)

Sunrise: 6:16 AM

Sunset: 5:32 PM

From these, it is obvious that the sun has risen earlier on June 22 compared to December 22 (5:28 AM vs. 6:16 AM). But it can be seen on the data that even though June 22 has earlier sunrise, it has late sunset compared to December 22 (6:28 PM vs. 5:32 PM).

We can conclude that the day time during the second quarter of the year (April to June) where the month of June belongs to is longer than the fourth quarter of the year (October to December) where the month of December belongs to.

14. sunrise is to dawn as sunset is to​


The term "dawn" is synonymous with the start of morning twilight. "Sunrise" occurs the moment the disc of the sun peeks above the eastern horizon due to the Earth's rotation. "Sunset" is the opposite. It occurs the moment the disc of sun completely disappears below the western horizon.
