7. our school has a circular loan with a radius of 25 meters.what is the area ​


Answer:

The area of a circle is given by the formula A = πr^2, where r is the radius of the circle and π (pi) is a mathematical constant approximately equal to 3.14.

Substituting the given radius of 25 meters into the formula, we get:

A = π(25)^2

A = π(625)

A ≈ 1,956.25 square meters

Therefore, the area of the circular loan with a radius of 25 meters is approximately 1,956.25 square meters.
