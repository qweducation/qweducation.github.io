28. 9. Suppose Anna, in a normal menstrual cycle, had her period on the 6th of February 2021st. When will her next day 1 be? A. February 18, after 12 days B. February 28, after 24 days C. March 5, after 28 days D. March 7, after 30 days ​


Assuming a normal menstrual cycle length of 28 days, Anna's next period should start approximately 28 days after the start of her last period on February 6, 2021.

To calculate the date of her next day 1, we can add 28 days to February 6, 2021:

February 6 + 28 days = March 6, 2021

Therefore, Anna's next day 1 will most likely be on March 6, 2021.

None of the answer choices match this date exactly, but option C (March 5, after 28 days) is the closest. However, it is only off by one day, so it may be a typo.
