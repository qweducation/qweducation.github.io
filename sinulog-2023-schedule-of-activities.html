2. What standard schedule will be followed for school yr. 2022-2023 as we embark on flexible learning?​

3. This 2023 how are you going to prioritize needs and activities ​


Answer:

Prioritizing needs and activities can be a helpful way to manage your time and ensure that you are able to accomplish what is most important to you. Here are some tips for prioritizing your needs and activities:

- Make a list of your needs and activities. This can help you see everything that you need to do and consider which items are most important.

- Identify your priorities. Consider what is most important to you and what will have the biggest impact on your life. These are likely to be your top priorities.

- Consider deadlines. If something has a deadline, it may need to be a higher priority.

- Evaluate the impact of each need or activity. Consider how each item on your list will affect you and your long-term goals.

- Use time management tools. There are many tools and techniques that can help you prioritize your tasks and manage your time effectively.

Remember, it is okay to have multiple priorities and to adjust your priorities as your needs and circumstances change. The key is to be proactive and intentional about how you spend your time and energy.
