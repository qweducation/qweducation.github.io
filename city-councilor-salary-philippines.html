12. Identify our local administrator and official in Tarlac City government (from the 10 City Councilor to Vice Mayor to City Mayor). Enumerate their powers of functions.​


As of September 2021, here are the local administrators and officials in Tarlac City government and their respective powers and functions:

1.City Mayor: Cristy Angeles

The City Mayor is the highest ranking official in the city government and is responsible for managing the city's affairs, ensuring the delivery of basic services to the people, and promoting the general welfare of the constituents. Some of the City Mayor's powers and functions include:

Enforcing laws and ordinancesFormulating policies and programs for the cityApproving or vetoing ordinances and resolutions passed by the City CouncilSigning contracts and agreements on behalf of the cityPreparing and submitting the annual budget of the cityAppointing officials and employees in the city government

2.Vice Mayor: Genaro Mendoza

The Vice Mayor is the second highest ranking official in the city government and serves as the presiding officer of the City Council. The Vice Mayor's powers and functions include:

Presiding over City Council sessions and maintaining order and decorumVoting on City Council matters in case of a tieAssuming the duties and responsibilities of the City Mayor in case of absence, incapacity, or vacancyRepresenting the city in various functions and events

3.City Councilors:

The City Council is composed of 10 members who are elected by the people. They are responsible for enacting ordinances and resolutions, approving the annual budget, and conducting inquiries in aid of legislation. Some of the City Councilors' powers and functions include:

Introducing and sponsoring ordinances and resolutionsApproving or rejecting ordinances and resolutionsConducting hearings and investigations in aid of legislationExercising oversight functions over city officials and employeesParticipating in the budget deliberations and approval process.

28. ano ang katungkulan ng isang city councilors ​


Answer:

Ang mga Konsehal ay bumubuo sa Sangguniang Panglungsod, ito ang sangay ng lehislatura ng isang siyudad, ito ay binubuo ng Bise alkalde bilang Pangulo. It is composed of the vice mayor, the president of the municipal or city chapter of the league of barangays, the president of the federation of Sangguniang Kabataan (youth council), regular sanggunian members (councilors), and sectoral representatives.

The regular members and sectoral representatives of a city or town council are elected in the same election as the mayor or vice mayor.

Their tasks include the approval of ordinances and resolutions relating to peace and order, fines, penalties, appropriation, salaries, and the finances of the municipal or city government.

They are likewise responsible for the generation and use of resources and revenues and budget; the grant of franchises and issuance of permits; and the regulation of the use of land, buildings, and structures.
