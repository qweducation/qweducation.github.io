20. what do you think will happen if there will be no music in tv dramas, telenovelas, and movies that we are watching?​


Answer:

If there was no music in TV dramas, telenovelas, and movies, it would significantly impact the overall experience for viewers. Music is an essential component of the audio-visual medium, and it plays a crucial role in enhancing emotions, setting the tone, and creating a sense of atmosphere and tension. Without music, scenes that are supposed to be dramatic, romantic, or suspenseful would lose their impact and become less engaging for the audience.

Furthermore, music helps to reinforce the emotional connection that viewers have with the characters and the story. The right music can help viewers to empathize with the characters, understand their emotions and motivations, and become more invested in the narrative. Without music, these connections would be weakened, and viewers may feel less engaged or invested in the story.

Overall, the absence of music in TV dramas, telenovelas, and movies would lead to a less compelling and less emotionally engaging experience for the audience.
