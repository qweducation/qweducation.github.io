29. a car moves at all Constant speed of 80 kilometers per hours how much distance and covered if it travel 5hours and 6hours and 7hours​


Answer:

If a car moves at a constant speed of 80 kilometers per hour, then the distance it covers can be calculated using the formula:

distance = speed x time

Where speed is in kilometers per hour and time is in hours.

If the car travels for 5 hours, then the distance it covers is:

distance = 80 km/h x 5 h = 400 km

If the car travels for 6 hours, then the distance it covers is:

distance = 80 km/h x 6 h = 480 km

If the car travels for 7 hours, then the distance it covers is:

distance = 80 km/h x 7 h = 560 km

Therefore, the car will cover a distance of 400 km, 480 km, and 560 km if it travels for 5 hours, 6 hours, and 7 hours respectively at a constant speed of 80 km/h.
