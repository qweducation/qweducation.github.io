9. write down the procedure in checking open circuit in stop/brake light​


Answer:

The procedure for checking the open circuit at stop / brake light are as follows:

Start by doing a visual check on the lights. Get a helper to operate the brake pedal (with the ignition turned on). ...Next check the bulbholder. ...If the current reaches the output side of the fuse, suspect a fault in the wiring on the brake switch or switch itself.
