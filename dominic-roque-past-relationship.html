1. REFLECTION Give your own examples of one relationship of domination and one relationship of possession. Explain the elements that make the relationship a relationship of domination or possession.


Answer

1) Relationship of domination: A boss and their employee. The boss has power and authority over the employee, and the employee must follow the boss's orders and directives. The boss has the ability to hire, fire, and discipline the employee, and the employee's livelihood and career advancement depend on the boss's approval. This relationship is one of domination because the boss holds a position of power and control over the employee.

2) Relationship of possession: A pet owner and their animal. The pet owner has the legal right to possess the animal and make decisions on its behalf, including where it will live and what it will eat. The animal is dependent on the owner for care and survival, and the owner has the ability to sell or give away the animal if they choose. This relationship is one of possession because the owner holds legal ownership over the animal and has control over its well-being.

Answer:

Si Heneral Eusebio Roque ay isang magiting na katipunero na lumaban sa mga Kastila noong 1897.

Explanation:

Noong nagsimula ang laban ng mga katipunero sa  mga Kastila, pinamunuaan ito ni Andres Bonifacio at may naganap ng laban sa San Juan Del Monte. Ngunit natalo ang pwersa dito. Dahil sa natalo ay pinanghinaan ng loob ang ibang katipunero lalo na sa mga nasa Central Luzon.
