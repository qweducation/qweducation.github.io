1. 9. The Panguil Bay Bridge, as well as several other bridges, (is, are) among the "Build,Build Build” projects of the Duterte administration.


Answer:

is

Explanation:

A rule in Subject-Verb Agreement to always remember:

Phrases like as well as, together with, and along with does NOT compound the subject or change it to another form (singular/plural).

Example:

Wrong - The students, along with their adviser, is having fun at the field trip.

Correct - The students, along  with their adviser, are having fun at the field trip.

In this example we should remember that the adviser (which is introduced by the phrase along with) will not modify if the verb is singular or plural. Since the phrase, "The students" is the earlier word/phrase, it will be the one modifying or determining if the verb is singular or plural.

13. A train loaded with fuel, 120m long, travels from Laguna de Bay to Manila Bay, with a constant speed of 50 km/h. This train takes 15s to completely cross the bridge over the Pasig River. The bridge length is:​


The bridge is 88.3m long.

Hi! To resolve this issue, we must take into account that for the train to completely cross the bridge, it needs to completely cross the bridge, that is, exceed its 120m plus the distance from the bridge.

As it has a speed of 50 km/h (or 13.9 m/s) and takes 15 s, we have:

Vm = space/time

13.9 = space/15s

Space = 208.3m

We will have that the size of the bridge is given by the entire route traveled (208.3m) minus the size of the truck (120m), which gives us a value of 88.3 meters.
