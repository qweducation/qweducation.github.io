10. A plumber charges 55 for a service call plus 35 per hour. If the plumbers bill was 160, how many hours was he there


Answer:

3 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the number of hours

35x + 55 = 160 (Find x)

Answer:

The plumber worked for 3 hours.

Step-by-step explanation:

Flat/Basic charges:  55

Additional charges: 35 per hour

Hour: x

Total Bill: f(x) = 160

Equation:

f(x) = 55 + 35x

Solve for number of hours, x:

160 = 55 + 35x

35x = 160 - 55

35x/35 = 105/35

x = 3

The plumber worked for 3 hours.
