29. a teacher began her services at a salary of 25,000 a year and received an annual increase of 800 for six years how much did she receive on her seventh year?​


Answer:

The teacher received 29,800 on her 7th year.

Step-by-step explanation:

On her first year, she received 25,000. Every year she gets an increase of 800 to her salary.

1st year: 25,0002nd year: 25,000 + 800 = 25,8003rd year: 25,800 + 800 = 26, 6004th year: 26,600 + 800 = 27, 4005th year: 27,400 + 800 = 28, 2006th year: 28,200 + 800 = 29,0007th year: 29,000 + 800 = 29,800

You can make this in a tabular form to easily see the increase for each year.

This is an example of arithmetic sequence where the next number on the sequence is obtained by adding a constant number, in this case 800, to the previous number.

To solve for the teacher's salary for nth year, where n is the number of years, we can use the formula:

25, 000 + (n-1)800 = Salary for the nth year.
