22. jenny buys a casio calculator for 720 pesos and sells it at a loss of 6 2/3 %. For how much does she sell it?​


Answer:

₱672

Step-by-step explanation:

The price of the calculator is ₱720

Jenny sells it at a loss of 6⅔%

[tex] \implies 720-6\frac{2}{3}\%[/tex]

[tex]\implies 720-\frac{20}{3}\%[/tex]

[tex]\implies 720-\frac{1}{15}[/tex]

[tex]\implies720-\left(720\times\frac{1}{15}\right)[/tex]

[tex]\implies720-\left(\frac{720}{15}\right)[/tex]

[tex]\implies720-48=672[/tex]

Then she sells it at ₱672
