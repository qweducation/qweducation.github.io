1. Company A offer a clerk a starting salary of 3400 with an incentive of a 200 salary increase every month will be the clerk's salary after 3 years?


3400 base salary

3 years is 36 months

200(monthly salary increase)x36(months)
=7200

7200+3400= 10600 the salary of the clerk in 3 years.Use the formula for sum of arithmetic sequence.

[tex] S_{n} [/tex] = n/2 [2(a₁) + (n-1) (d)]

Convert 3 years to months:  3 x 12 = 36 (months)

n = 36
a₁ = 3 400
d = 200

Solution:
S₃₆ = 36/2 [2 (3400) + (36-1)(200)]
S₃₆ = 18 [6800 + (35)(200)]
S₃₆ = 18 [6800 + 7000]
S₃₆ = 18 [13,800]
S₃₆ = 248,400   

ANSWER:  The clerk will receive a salary of 248,400 after 3 years.

Answer:

To determine if the clerk made a mistake, we can calculate the total cost of the fabric based on the given information.

Lauren bought 3 m of fabric at PhP 53.50 per meter, so the total cost should be:

Total cost = 3 meters x PhP 53.50/meter

Total cost = PhP 160.50

Based on this calculation, it appears that the clerk did make a mistake. The clerk charged Lauren PhP 149.50, which is PhP 11.00 less than the actual total cost of the fabric.

Therefore, it would be a good idea for Lauren to double-check her receipt and speak with the clerk to correct the mistake and ensure that she is charged the correct amount.

23. Clara Ramos works as a sales clerk. She is paid a salary of P3000.00 a week plus 2% commission an sales over P4000.00. Find her gross pay for a week in which her sales amounts to P9500.00.


The gross salary Clara received was P3.190,00.

Step-by-step explanation:

Be discovered:

Clara Ramos works as a sales clerk. She is paid a salary of P3000,00 a week plus 2% commission an sales over P4000,00.

Question:

Find her gross pay for a week in which her sales amounts to P9500,00.

Asked:

Because the number of sales exceeds P4000, Clara gets a 2% bonus from the total sales.

The first step, we look for the big bonus that Clara received.

Bonus received by Clara = Bonus percentage times the number of sales

Bonus received by Clara = 2% x P9500,00

Bonus received by Clara = P190,00

The second step is to find the amount of gross salary that Clara receives.

Clara's gross salary = base salary + bonus

Clara's gross salary = P3000,00 + P190,00

Clara's gross salary = P3.190,00

The gross salary Clara received was P3.190,00.
