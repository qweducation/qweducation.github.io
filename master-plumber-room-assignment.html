6. a master painter can paint the room in 5 hours,while his apprentice can do the same job in 7 hours.In how many hours will paint the room if they work together?​


Answer:

They can paint the room in 2.94 Hours.

Step-by-step explanation:

[tex] \\ \sf{} \frac{x}{5} + \frac{x}{7} = 1 [/tex]

LCD is 35. Multiply all the terms by the LCD.

[tex] \\ \sf{}35 \bigg( \frac{x}{5} + \frac{x}{7} = 1 \bigg)[/tex]

[tex] \\ \sf{} \frac{35x}{5} + \frac{35x}{7} = 35[/tex]

[tex] \\ \sf{}7x + 5x = 35[/tex]

[tex] \\ \sf{}12x = 35[/tex]

[tex] \\ \sf{}x = \frac{35}{12} [/tex]

[tex] \\ \sf{}x = 2.91666666667[/tex]

[tex] \\ \huge \boxed{ \sf{}x = 2.92} \\ \\ \\ \\ \\ [/tex]

18. A plumber charges 55 for a service call plus 35 per hour. If the plumbers bill was 160, how many hours was he there


Answer:

3 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the number of hours

35x + 55 = 160 (Find x)

Answer:

The plumber worked for 3 hours.

Step-by-step explanation:

Flat/Basic charges:  55

Additional charges: 35 per hour

Hour: x

Total Bill: f(x) = 160

Equation:

f(x) = 55 + 35x

Solve for number of hours, x:

160 = 55 + 35x

35x = 160 - 55

35x/35 = 105/35

x = 3

The plumber worked for 3 hours.
