20. mike is twice as old cristy cristy is 1/4 Remys age Remy is 1/2 bens age is 8 years old how old i mike​


Answer:

Let's start by finding the age of Ben:

Since Remy is half of Ben's age, we can write:

Remy's age = 1/2 * Ben's age

And we know that Ben is 8 years old, so:

Remy's age = 1/2 * 8 = 4 years old

Now we can use the information given about Cristy's age to find Mike's age:

Cristy's age = 1/4 * Remy's age

Substituting Remy's age from above, we get:

Cristy's age = 1/4 * 4 = 1 year old

We also know that Mike is twice as old as Cristy, so:

Mike's age = 2 * Cristy's age = 2 * 1 = 2 years old

Therefore, Mike is 2 years old.
