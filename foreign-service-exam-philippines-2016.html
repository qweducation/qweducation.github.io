14. Ano ang foreign trade service corps


Answer:

Ang Foreign Trade Service Corps (FTSC) ay binubuo ng Mga Kinatawan sa Pangangalakal o Komersyong Komersyal na kumakatawan sa Komersyal na  Kagawaran ng Kalakal at Industriya (DTI) sa buong mundo.

Itinataguyod ng FTSC ang mga inisyatibo ng DTI, partikular sa promosyong pangkalakal at pamumuhunan, pati na rin ang patakaran sa kalakalan sa mga pangunahing merkado sa ibang bansa.

Bilang bahagi ng DTI's Trade and Investments Promotion Group, ang FTSC ay nagtatrabaho sa buong mundo upang ikonekta ang mga kumpanya at mamumuhunan sa Pilipinas sa kanilang mga katapat sa ibang bansa.

18. On July 1, 2015, Hanson Company borrowed 200,000 foreign currencies (FC's) from a foreign lender evidenced by an interest-bearing note due on July 1, 2016. The note is denominated in foreign currencies. The Philippine peso equivalent of the note principal is as follows: Date Amount July 1, 2015 (date borrowed) P1950.000 December 31, 2015(Hanson’s year end) 220,000 July 1 2016 (date repaid) 230,000 In its 2016 income statement, what amount should Hanson include as a foreign exchange gain or loss on the note? A. P35,000 gain B. P35,000 loss C. P10,000 gain D. P10,000 loss show solution& short explanation


Answer:

Explanation:

Solution:

To determine the foreign exchange gain or loss on the note, we need to compute for the difference between the peso amount paid upon repayment of the note and the peso value of the note when it was borrowed.

Peso value of the note when borrowed = P1,950,000

Peso value of the note when repaid = P230,000

Foreign exchange gain/loss = P230,000 - P1,950,000 = (P1,720,000)

Since the foreign exchange gain/loss is negative, it means that Hanson has a foreign exchange loss of P1,720,000. However, this amount needs to be adjusted for the interest expense incurred on the note.

Interest expense = P220,000 x 12/12 x 6% = P13,200

Adjusted foreign exchange loss = P1,720,000 - P13,200 = (P1,706,800)

Therefore, the answer is B. P35,000 loss.

Short Explanation:

To compute for the foreign exchange gain or loss on a note denominated in foreign currencies, we compare the peso value of the note when it was borrowed and when it was repaid. If the peso value when repaid is higher than when borrowed, there is a gain. If it is lower, there is a loss. We also need to adjust for any interest expense incurred on the note.

26. What are the main functions of Foreign Trade Service Corps? ​


Foreign trade service crops provide many functions, including:

1. Promotion of international business: Foreign trade service crops assist in promoting international business by facilitating communication and negotiation between countries. They provide a platform for international companies to showcase their products and services, and connect with potential customers.

2. Market analysis and research: Foreign trade service crops gather information about international markets, consumer trends, and emerging economies. This data helps companies make informed decisions about which markets to enter and how to position their products or services.

3. Export assistance: Foreign trade service crops provide guidance and assistance to exporters, including information about taxes, customs regulations, and duties. They also help exporters comply with legal requirements and navigate the complexities of international trade.

4. Import assistance: Foreign trade service crops also assist companies that import goods from overseas. They provide information about international suppliers, negotiate prices and contracts, and assist with customs clearance.

5. Trade promotion: Foreign trade service crops work to promote trade between countries, including organizing trade missions, conferences, and exhibitions. They also provide assistance with trade financing and insurance.

In summary, foreign trade service crops perform a crucial function in facilitating international business and promoting global trade.

29. Ano ang kahulugan ng civil service exam


Pagsusulit sa Serbisyo Sibil o Civil Service Exam.

Ang Pagsusulit sa Serbisyo Sibil ay isang pagsusuri na ginagamit upang subukan ang pagiging karapat-dapat ng mga taong nag-aaplay para sa ilang mga tungkulin sa mga trabaho sa serbisyo sibil.

Ang mga pagsusulit ay tumutulong sa pag-screen sa mga taong walang kakayahan para sa trabaho. Ang mga pagsusulit ay nag-iiba depende sa sektor at antas ng pamahalaan na nag-aalok ng trabaho.

Mga Bentahe ng Civil Service Examination:

Ang mga trabahong ito ay kadalasang nag-aalok ng malalaking suweldo, benepisyo, at pangmatagalang katatagan ng karera, at ang pinakamahuhusay na kandidato ay may praktikal na kasanayan at gustong magbigay pabalik sa kanilang mga komunidad sa pamamagitan ng trabaho sa gobyerno. Ang pagkuha ng pagsusulit sa serbisyo sibil ay madalas na unang hadlang sa pagkuha ng trabaho sa serbisyo sibil.

Mga kinakailangan para sa pagkuha ng pagsusulit sa serbisyo sibil:

Mamamayang Pilipino,

Maging hindi bababa sa 18 taong gulang, at magkaroon ng magandang moral na karakter.

Walang kriminal na rekord, o hindi kailanman nahatulan ng panghuling paghatol para sa isang pagkakasala o krimen na kinasasangkutan ng isang paglabag sa moral.

Pagsusuri ng career path sa loob ng 3 (tatlong) buwan mula nang isagawa ang huling pagsusuri.
