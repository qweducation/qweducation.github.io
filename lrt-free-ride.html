To find the answers in the questions above, we have to use a Venn diagram. The Venn diagram is a visual representation of two or more classifications and numbers in the form of overlapping circles.

Let us analyze the given problem using the Venn diagram. I will attach the picture of the Venn diagram for this problem.

students who had ridden the busstudents who had ridden the LRTstudents who had ridden the jeep

Bus: 68LRT: 78Jeep: 33

40 had ridden both the bus and the LRT: Since we already have 15 in the overlap of bus and LRT, we have to subtract then write the answer in the remaining part of the overlap. 40 - 15 = 25

20 had ridden the bus and the jeep: Since we already have 15 in the overlap of bus and jeep, we have to subtract then write the answer in the remaining part of the overlap. 40 - 15 = 5

19 had ridden the LRT and the jeep: Since we already have 15 in the overlap of LRT and jeep, we have to subtract then write the answer in the remaining part of the overlap. 19 - 15 = 4

68 - (25 + 15 + 5)

= 68 - 45

= 23

b.) How many had ridden the LRT only?

78 - (25 + 15 + 4)

= 78 - 44

= 34

c.) How many had ridden the jeep only?

33 - (5 + 15 + 4)

= 33 - 24

= 9

d.) How many did not ride on any of the three modes of transportation?
