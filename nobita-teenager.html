1. Doraemon can run 10 kilometers in the same amount time that Nobita can 12kilometers. If Nobita can run 1 kilometers per hour faster than Doraemon, how fast canNobita run? ASAP​


Answer:

6 km/hr

Step-by-step explanation:

Formula:

d = st

t = d/s

where:

d = distance

s = speed

t = time

For Doraemon:

d1 = 10 km

s1 = x (unknown)

For Nobita:

d2 = 12 km

s2 = x + 1 ( 1 km/hr faster than Doraemon)

since t1 = t2

d1/s1 = d2/s2

10/x = 12/(x+1) [cross multiply]

10(x+1) = 12x

10x + 10 = 12x

12x - 10x = 10

2x = 10

x = 5 km/hr

s2 = x + 1 = 5 + 1 = 6

therefore, Nobita can run at 6 km/hr
