Answer:

Ask : How many cups of cherries will Christine need?

Given : 9 2/7 cups of cherries, 6 cherry pies

Operation : Multiplication

Number Snetence : 9 2/7 × 6 = N

Solution :

9 2/7 × 6 = N

7 × 9 = 63

63 + 2 = 65

65/7 × 6 = N

65 × 6 = 390

7 × 6 = 42

390/42 = N

= 9 12/42

Answer w/ Label : 9 12/42 cups of cherries
