Germinal disc sometimes called blastodisc is the entrance of the latebra, a small circular white spot on the surface of the yolk and a the channel leading to the center of the yolk. Shell is the hard outer case of the egg.


2. this is the entrance of the latebra


Answer:

Germinal Disc-The entrance of the latebra, the channel leading to the center of the yolk. The germinal disc is barely noticeable as a slight depression on the surface of the yolk. When the egg is fertilized, sperm enter by way of the germinal disc, travel to the center and a chick embryo starts to form.

28. This is the entrance of the latebra, the channel leading to the center of the yolk.a. chalaza b. germinal disc c. shell d. yolk


Answer:

B. Germinal Disc
