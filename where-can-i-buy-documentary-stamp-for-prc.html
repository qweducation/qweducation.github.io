3. I bought some P5-stamps and P13-stamps worth a total of P123. If I bought 15 stamps, how many of each kind did I buy?


You bought nine ₱5 and six ₱13

Let:
x be the number of pieces of P5 stamps
y be the number of pieces of P13 stamps

5x + 13y = 123
x + y = 15 => x = 15 - y

5x + 13y = 123
5(15 - y) + 13y = 123
75 - 5y + 13y = 123
-5y + 13y = 123 - 75
8y = 48
8y/8 = 48/8
y = 6

x + y = 15
x + 6 = 15
x = 15 - 6
x = 9

Therefore, there are 6 pieces of P13 stamp and 9 pieces of P5 stamp.

Checking:
5x + 13y = 123
5(9) + 13(6) = 123
45 + 78 = 123
123 = 123
