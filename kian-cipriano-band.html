24. Kian is twice as old as Xhendric. The sum of their ages is 36. How old are they?


Answer:

Kian is 24 years old while Xhendric is 12 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let:

Xhendric's age = x

Kian's age = 2x

Equation:

[tex]x + 2x = 36[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]x + 2x = 36 \\ 3x = 36 \\ \frac{3x}{3} = \frac{36}{3} \\ x = 12[/tex]

Finding Kian's age:

[tex]2x \\ 2(12) \\ 24[/tex]
