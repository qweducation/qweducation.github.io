1. Explenation of Cebu Metropolitan Cathedral​


Answer:

The Cebu Metropolitan Cathedral is the ecclesiastical seat of the Metropolitan Archdiocese of Cebu in Cebu, Philippines. It was established as a diocese on August 14, 1595 and elevated as a metropolitan archdiocese on April 28, 1934 with the dioceses of Dumaguete, Maasin, Tagbilaran, and Talibon as suffrages.

13. michael scheduled a trip to cebu on Christmas season. the plane flew at an average rate of 480 km/h and after travelling 2 850 km, his plane touched down what was michael's travel time from manila to cebu​


PROBLEM:

Michael scheduled a trip to cebu on Christmas season. The plane flew at an average rate of 480 km/h and after travelling 2 850 km, his plane touched down. What was michael's travel time from manila to cebu?

[tex] \blue {\overline{ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: }} [/tex]

SOLUTION:

• In the problem, the average rate or what we call the speed and distance travelled are known while the time taken is not given.

• We'll use the formula [tex] \rm Speed=Distance÷Time[/tex] to solve for the unknown quantity.

[tex] \rm Speed=Distance÷Time[/tex]

[tex] \rm 480 \: \frac{km}{h} = 2850 \: km \div t[/tex]

[tex] \rm \frac{480 \: km}{h} = \frac{ 2850 \: km } t \\ [/tex]

[tex] \rm 480t \: km = 2850 \: kmh[/tex]

[tex] \rm \frac{480t \: km}{480 \: km} = \frac{2850 \: kmh}{480 \: km} \\ [/tex]

[tex] \huge \boxed{ \large \rm t = 5.94 \: h}[/tex]

[tex] \blue {\overline{ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: }} [/tex]

ANSWER:Michael's travel time from Manila to Cebu is 5.94 hours.
