10. In the class of Mrs Casio 5pupils can finish their project in 18 days At the same rate how many days can 10 pupils finish the same project?​


Answer:

9 days

Step-by-step explanation:

This is the application of variation.

The more students the shorter it takes for the job to do. This means that the number of students is indirectly proportional to the number of days to do the work.

Let k be the proportionality constant.

let P be the number of pupils

let D be the number of days.

P=k/D

k=PD

k=90

Given P=10,

P=k/D

D=k/P

D=90/10

D=9 days

19. jenny buys a casio calculator for 720 pesos and sells it at a loss of 6 2/3 %. For how much does she sell it?​


Answer:

₱672

Step-by-step explanation:

The price of the calculator is ₱720

Jenny sells it at a loss of 6⅔%

[tex] \implies 720-6\frac{2}{3}\%[/tex]

[tex]\implies 720-\frac{20}{3}\%[/tex]

[tex]\implies 720-\frac{1}{15}[/tex]

[tex]\implies720-\left(720\times\frac{1}{15}\right)[/tex]

[tex]\implies720-\left(\frac{720}{15}\right)[/tex]

[tex]\implies720-48=672[/tex]

Then she sells it at ₱672
