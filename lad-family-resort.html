23. Reyes family goes to a resort and drives for 2 hours at the speed of 80km/h. What distance did they travel?


To find the distance traveled by the Reyes family, we can use the formula:

distance = speed × time

where speed is measured in kilometers per hour (km/h) and time is measured in hours.

Substituting the given values, we get:

distance = 80 km/h × 2 hours

distance = 160 kilometers

Therefore, the Reyes family traveled a distance of 160 kilometers during their 2-hour drive to the resort at a speed of 80 km/h.
