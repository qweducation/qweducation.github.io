Given that

D=unknown

R=800meters per day

T=5 days

SUBSTITUTING THE VALUES TO THE FORMULA

D=800(5)D=4000 mD= 4km

29. Ravi bought 60 pens at 3 for $2. He sold all of them at 5 for $6.How much did she earn?​


Answer:

$32

Step-by-step explanation:

First, we need to find the cost of the pens.

Given that there are 60 pens, she availed the promo of "3 for $2" 20 times. Thus, she paid $40 for all 60 pens.

Now, we need to find her sales. This is all the money she got from selling the pens.

Given again that there are 60 pens, the promo "5 for $6" must be availed 12 times. Thus, she got $72.

Lastly, to find her profit, we need to get the difference of the two numbers.

$72 - $40 = $32.

Thus, she earned $32.
