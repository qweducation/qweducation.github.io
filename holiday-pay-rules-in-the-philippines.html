1. what is holiday pay?​


Holiday pay is any form of alternative compensation an employer offers employees during holidays. It may take the form of fully or partially paid time off or of a bonus or additional hourly pay for work performed on a holiday.

21. cultural holiday and ceremony in philippines​


Answer: Christmas

Explanation: People in the Philippines like to celebrate Christmas for as long as possible! The playing of Christmas carols in shops can start in September!
