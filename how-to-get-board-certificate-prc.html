6. how certificate of assignment works in banking?


Answer:

Certificate of assignment is a legal document that a bank may use to transfer ownership of a loan obligation from one person or entity to another. The certificate of assignment provides evidence that the bank has assigned and transferred its rights to receive payment on the loan to the new owner. The new owner can then take legal action to enforce the loan, if necessary, and collect any outstanding payments. The certificate of assignment is typically recorded with the relevant government agency to ensure that the new owner's rights are properly documented and protected.

16. ano ang tungkulin ng prc


The Professional Regulation Commission, (Filipino: Komisyon sa Regulasyon ng mga Propesyon) otherwise known as the PRC, is a three-man commission attached to Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE). Its mandate is to regulate and supervise the practice of the professionals (except lawyers, who are handled by the Supreme Court of the Philippines) who constitute the highly skilled manpower of the country. As the agency-in-charge of the professional sector, the PRC plays a strategic role in developing the corps of professionals for industry, commerce, governance, and the economy.

28. How to file a petition for cancellation of birth certificate?


PETITION FOR CANCELLATION OR CORRECTION OF ENTRIES IN THE CIVIL REGISTRY

1. Contact a lawyer who will prepare the Petition for Correction of Entry.

2. Lawyers shall file the Petition in the Regional Trial Court. The fee for filing the Petition in the Regional Trial Court is Php 160.00.

3. The Petition will be raffled and assigned to a branch of the Regional Trial Court.

4. The assigned Regional Trial Court shall issue an Order for the publication of its Order in a newspaper of general cirsulation for three consecutive weeks. Fee for publication varies.

5. The Order contains the date of the first hearing.

6. During the first hearing, the lawyer will present compliance of the jurisdictional requirements like publication of the Order in a newspaper of general circulation.

7. After establishing compliance with jurisdictional requirements, the petitioner will be presented in Court to testify. The Court may assign the Clerk of Court to receive evidence.

8. During the hearing, it is possible that an oppositor may appear contesting the Petition. If an oppositor appears, the Clerk of Court will receive the petitioner’s evidence.

9. After presentation of evidence, the Court will rule on the Petition.

10. If the decision is favorable, the Court will order the Office of Civil Registrar to correct the entry in the civil registry document (birth/marriage/death certificate of the petitioner).


29. Ano ang kahulugan ng PRC?


Ano ang Kahulugan ng PRC?

Ang kahulugan ng PRC ay Philippine Regulation Commission. Ito ay isang kagawaran ng gobyerno. Ito ang may hawak at nagbibigay ng mga pagsusulit para sa mga propesyonal ng bansa. Ang pagsusulit na ito ay tinatawag na board exam. Sila rin ang magiisyu ng mga lisensya nila pagkatapos nila matapos ang kanilang mga board exam.

Kung ibabase sa kahulugan ng PRC, mapapansin mo na agad dito ang ginagampanang tungkulin ng ahensyang ito:

magpatupad ng mga batas na siyang dapat sundin ng bawat propesyon at trabaho sa bansa

Kung ibabase sa batas, ang PRC ay mayroong dalawang tungkulin na ginagampanan sa ating bansa:

Mag-patupad ng mga pagsusulit o board exam para sa lisensya ng mga nais maging isang propesyonalMangasiwa sa mga propesyonal ng ating bansa sa kanilang paggampan sa kanilang kaniya kaniyang propesyon.
