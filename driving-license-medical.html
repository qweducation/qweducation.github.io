Obligations

Something that binds or obligates a person to do specific actions and which stems from a sense of duty, custom, law, etc. It's part of MODALS.

A requirement is a duty. It is your duty or obligation to call your mother on Mother's Day. Even better would be sending flowers.

When you are morally or legally obligated to fulfill a commitment, it is your responsibility to do so. For instance, if you observe a crime in progress, you are obligated to contact the police. If an elderly person boards a crowded bus, you are required to give up your seat for him.
