17. There are 140 senior citizens living in our barangay. that is 25% of the total population. How many people are not senior citizens?a.320b.380c.400d.420​


Answer:

D. 420

Step-by-step explanation:

First turn the percent into decimal form which make 25% = 0.25. After that, divide the 140 to 0.25 to know of total population.

140 ÷ 0.25 = 560

After that, subtract the 560 to 140 because the question is NOT SENIOR CITIZEN so-

560 - 140 = 420

FINAL ANSWER

420
