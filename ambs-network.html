13. Analyze and solve the following problems. 1. Line MQ intersects with line PN at point O. Also, ∠MON ≅ ∠POQ. If m∠NOQ = 30°, what is the measure of ∠MON? Solution and answer: 2. ∠AMB and ∠BMD forms a linear pair. Line segment MC bisects ∠BMD such that m∠CMD = 70°. Find the measure of ∠AMB.


Answer:

Since ∠MON ≅ ∠POQ, then line MQ is parallel to line PN. By the transversal rule, we have:

m∠NOQ + m∠QOP = 180°

Substituting m∠NOQ = 30°, we have:

30° + m∠QOP = 180°

m∠QOP = 150°

Since ∠MON and ∠POQ are alternate interior angles, they are congruent. Therefore,

m∠MON = m∠POQ = 150°.

Answer: The measure of ∠MON is 150°.

Since ∠AMB and ∠BMD form a linear pair, we have:

m∠AMB + m∠BMD = 180°

Since line MC bisects ∠BMD, we have:

m∠CMD = m∠BMD/2

Substituting m∠CMD = 70°, we have:

m∠BMD/2 = 70°

m∠BMD = 140°

Substituting m∠AMB + 140° = 180°, we have:

m∠AMB = 40°

Answer: The measure of ∠AMB is 40°.
