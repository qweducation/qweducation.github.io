Answer:

DPWH Infrastructure Projects

Ang DPWH ay ang Department of Public Works and Highways sa Pilipinas. Ito ay isang ahensya ng pamahalaan na mayroong mga proyekto para sa imprastraktura sa buong bansa. Ang kanilang mga proyekto ay kinabibilangan ng mga sumusunod:

Konstruksyon ng mga kalsada, tulay, at iba pang mga imprastraktura tulad ng airports at seaports.

Pagpapanatili at pagpapabuti ng mga kalsada at tulay upang masiguro ang kaligtasan ng mga motorista at naglalakbay.

Pagpapagawa ng mga flood control system upang maiwasan ang pagbaha.

Pagpapatayo ng mga gusali at pasilidad tulad ng mga paaralan, ospital, at iba pang mga pangangailangan ng komunidad.

Sa pangkalahatan, ang DPWH ay may mga proyekto na may layuning mapabuti ang imprastraktura sa Pilipinas upang mas mapabilis ang pag-unlad ng ekonomiya at makapagbigay ng magandang serbisyo sa publiko.

25. Let’s assume you're in charge of hiring or the hiring manager. What qualities (at least 5) do you seek for in a candidate who wants to work as a receptionist at the front office? Make a list of attributes and explain why you selected those.​


Answer:

1. Organizational skills

2. Time Management skills

3. Computer skills

4. Pleasant Personality

5. Communication skills

Explanation:

1. Organizational skills are needed because a receptionist needs to handle multiple tasks as soon as they pop up. He or she needs to organizd these tasks to effectively complete them.

2. Time management skills go hand in hand with organizational skills. Time management is crucial for scheduling activities and interviews.

3. Computer skills are necessary so that the receptionist can encode data that they receive from guests.

4. A pleasing personality is crucial because the front desk receptionist is the face of the company. How he or she deals with guests and clients is crucial to the image of the company.

5. Communication skills are essential because a receptionist will be dealing with a lot of people.
