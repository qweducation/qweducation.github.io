8. The name of Rajah Humabon’s wife, when she became a queen.


Humani was the name of Rajah Humabon's wife when she became queen. But on April 14, 1521, after being converted to Christianity together with her husband and some 800 Cebuanos, Humani was given the name Queen Juana in honor of the mother of King Charles. The Rajah, on the other hand, took the name Carlos after King Charles himself.

There are some records saying that Reyna Juana's birth name is Hara Humamay and that she was actually Rajah Humabon's chief consort.

The taking on of a new name, especially a Christian name, is part of Christianity's baptismal customs. It signals a new identity as the person is born anew in Christ.
