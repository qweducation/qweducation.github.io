3. What is the difference between chief of the pnp and chief of police?​


Answer:

Chief of the PNP leads the Philippine National Police at the national level, while the Chief of Police leads at the local level.

Explanation:

The chief of the PNP (C, PNP) is the the head of the Philippine National Police. This person is responsible for the command and direction of the PNP at the national level. This position held by a Police General, which is a four-star general police officer. The current Chief of the Philippine National Police is PGen. Guillermo Eleazar who took the office in May 8, 2021.

The chief of police (COP) is the leader, supervisor, and administrator of the Philippine National Police's frontline unit: The Police Stations.
