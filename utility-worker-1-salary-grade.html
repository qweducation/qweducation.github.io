30. the salary of two workers is in the ratio 1:2. they received P9000 how much did each worker receive?


Answer:

The worker with the ratio of 1 received P 3000 and the other worker with the ratio of 2 received P 6000.

Step-by-step explanation:

To get each amount we multiply the total money by the worker's ratio and divide that by the sum of the ratios.

Example:

Worker with ratio 1

[tex]9000 \times \frac{1}{3 } = 3000[/tex]

Worker with ratio 2

[tex]9000 \times \frac{2}{3} = 6000[/tex]
