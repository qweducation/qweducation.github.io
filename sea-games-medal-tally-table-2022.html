Answer:

Kinetic Energy is 3.31 KJ

Explanation:

Note: What is the unit of velocity? If unit is in m/s, the computation is shown below. If the unit is not in m/s, convert the given unit to m/s and substitute it in the equation.

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

m = 50 kg

v = 11.5 m/s

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Kinetic Energy

Step 3: Solve for Kinetic Energy

Solution:

The formula for Kinetic Energy is given by:

KE = ½ mv²

where: KE - Kinetic Energy

m - mass

v - velocity

Substituting the value of m and v in the formula,

KE = (½)(50 kg)(11.5 m/s)²

PE = 3306.25 kgm²/s² or 3306.25 Nm or 3.30625 KJ

or 3.31 KJ
