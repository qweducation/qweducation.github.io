11. If you will be the one to handle the task for the development of tour guides in the Philippines what programs can you propose in order for our local tour guides to be more developed


Answer:

As someone who is tasked with developing tour guides in the Philippines, I believe that there are several programs that we can propose in order to enhance the skills and knowledge of our local tour guides. Here are some of my proposals:

1. Accreditation and Certification Program - This program aims to ensure that all tour guides in the country are qualified and trained to provide high-quality services to tourists. This program includes standardized training, assessments, and certifications for tour guides.

2. Continuing Education Program - This program aims to provide regular updates and training for tour guides to keep them up-to-date with the latest trends, information, and developments in the tourism industry. This can be done through workshops, seminars, and online courses.

3. Mentorship Program - This program aims to pair experienced and successful tour guides with new and aspiring tour guides. Through this program, new tour guides can learn from the experiences and expertise of their mentors and gain valuable insights and knowledge.

4. Cultural Immersion Program - This program aims to immerse tour guides in the local culture and history of the places they will be guiding tourists. Through this program, tour guides can gain a deeper understanding and appreciation of their local heritage, which they can then share with their guests.

5. Language Training Program - This program aims to provide language training for tour guides to improve their communication skills with foreign tourists. Tour guides will be trained to speak fluently in various languages commonly spoken by tourists such as English, Mandarin, and Korean.

By implementing these programs, I believe that we can develop a strong and competent pool of tour guides in the Philippines, which can lead to more positive tourism experiences for both local and foreign tourists.

A fish is not a reptile.

A bird is not a mammal.

BIRDS AND REPTILES

The difference between Fish and Reptiles is that fishes breathe through gills while reptiles do not have gills. They respire through the lungs itself. Their ribs can expand and contract which allows their lungs to have greater capacity than amphibians.  

Fish belongs are the largest existing vertebrates species. They belong to the Gnathostomata class. This is a subphylum group and a superclass of Chordata. They are all vertebrate species that have a structure jaw. Some common examples of fishes are clownfish, sharks, etc. They are the species of the animal kingdom that habitat in water.  

Reptiles belong to the class known as Reptilian. This class includes a variety are species such as turtles, snakes, lizards, etc. These animals are categorized under the Reptilian class because of their characteristic features such as cold-blooded, dry skin. They are commonly also known as the cold-blooded vertebrates that have epidermal glands.

BIRDS AND MAMMAL

The definition of a bird requires feathers, a toothless beak, wings (usually allowing for flight), and the ability to lay hard-shelled eggs. Meanwhile, mammals have hair, give birth to live young, and the females produce milk from mammary glands. the structures for which the class is named.

Whereas birds have feathers, lack teeth and lay eggs, mammals have fur or hair for insulation, possess teeth and give birth to live young. Although birds are more closely related to reptiles than mammals, birds and mammals have several characteristics in common.

Welcome to the enchanting archipelago of the Philippines, a tropical paradise that offers a plethora of breathtaking destinations and cultural experiences. Let me be your virtual tour guide as we embark on an unforgettable journey through some of the country's most captivating locations, starting with the bustling capital city of Manila.

Manila, a vibrant metropolis, blends modernity with rich history. Explore the walled city of Intramuros, where you can stroll along cobblestone streets and discover Spanish colonial architecture. Visit Fort Santiago, a citadel that holds significant historical value, or immerse yourself in the past at the National Museum of the Philippines. For shopaholics, indulge in retail therapy at the sprawling malls of Makati or experience the bustling markets of Divisoria.

As we venture outside Manila, let's head to Cebu, known as the Queen City of the South. Discover the fascinating blend of heritage and urbanization in this bustling city. For nature enthusiasts, a visit to Oslob is a must, where you can swim alongside whale sharks or take a dip in the refreshing Kawasan Falls.

Moving on, let's explore the pristine islands of Palawan. Palawan boasts unparalleled natural beauty, with its turquoise waters, limestone cliffs, and hidden lagoons. Explore the enchanting Underground River in Puerto Princesa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, or embark on an island-hopping adventure in El Nido, where you can discover secret beaches and stunning coral reefs. For a taste of paradise, Coron offers crystal-clear lakes, vibrant marine life, and picturesque shipwrecks for diving enthusiasts.

Another must-visit destination is the breathtaking Banaue Rice Terraces in the province of Ifugao. Explore the enchanting Philippine Tarsier Sanctuary, home to the world's smallest primates, or enjoy a leisurely cruise along the Loboc River.

Immerse yourself in the rich cultural tapestry of the Philippines by attending vibrant festivals such as the Ati-Atihan in Kalibo or the Sinulog Festival in Cebu. Experience the warm hospitality of the Filipino people as you indulge in traditional delicacies like adobo, lechon, and halo-halo.

With its breathtaking landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture, the Philippines offers an endless array of experiences that will leave you mesmerized. From the bustling streets of Manila to the idyllic islands of Palawan, this tropical paradise promises an unforgettable journey filled with adventure, beauty, and unforgettable memories.
