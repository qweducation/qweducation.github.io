14. ano ang tungkulin ng prc


The Professional Regulation Commission, (Filipino: Komisyon sa Regulasyon ng mga Propesyon) otherwise known as the PRC, is a three-man commission attached to Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE). Its mandate is to regulate and supervise the practice of the professionals (except lawyers, who are handled by the Supreme Court of the Philippines) who constitute the highly skilled manpower of the country. As the agency-in-charge of the professional sector, the PRC plays a strategic role in developing the corps of professionals for industry, commerce, governance, and the economy.

23. Give the different Genetic Engineering tool or processes, give the function and application of each.(GENETIC ENGINEERING)Tool/Processes:Restriction-EnzymesGelElectrophoresis-RecombinantDNA Technology-Gene Cloning-DNA Sequencing-Polymerase Chain-Reaction (PRC)​


Answer:

TOOL/PROCESSES;

RESTRICTION ENZYMES:

FUNCTION

-the process of cutting the DNA strands into fragments.

APPLICATION

-this is basically used to create DNA fragments with sticky ends or blunt ends that can join with other DNA fragments.

GEL ELECTROPHORESIS:

FUNCTION

-the process of separating DNA fragments by size.

APPLICATION

-this is used to study DNA fragments of various sizes.

RECOMBINANT DNA TECHNOLOGY:

FUNCTION

-the combination of a DNA fragments with the DNA from another source.

APPLICATION

-this is also called exogenous DNA.

GENE CLONING :

FUNCTION

-the production of the large numbers of identical recombinant DNA molecules.

APPLICATION

-used to create large amounts of recombinant DNA to be used in genetically engineered organism.

DNA SEQUENCING:

FUNCTION

-used to identify the DNA sequence of cloned recombinant DNA molecule to further study.

APPLICATION

-used to identify errors in the DNA sequences to predict the function of a particular gene and to compare to other genes with similar sequences from different organisms.

POLYMERASE CHAIN REACTION (PCR):

FUNTION

-used to make copies of specific regions of sequenced DNA.

APPLICATION

-used to copy DNA for any scientific investigation including forensic analysis and medical testing.

25. Ano ang kahulugan ng PRC?


Ano ang Kahulugan ng PRC?

Ang kahulugan ng PRC ay Philippine Regulation Commission. Ito ay isang kagawaran ng gobyerno. Ito ang may hawak at nagbibigay ng mga pagsusulit para sa mga propesyonal ng bansa. Ang pagsusulit na ito ay tinatawag na board exam. Sila rin ang magiisyu ng mga lisensya nila pagkatapos nila matapos ang kanilang mga board exam.

Kung ibabase sa kahulugan ng PRC, mapapansin mo na agad dito ang ginagampanang tungkulin ng ahensyang ito:

magpatupad ng mga batas na siyang dapat sundin ng bawat propesyon at trabaho sa bansa

Kung ibabase sa batas, ang PRC ay mayroong dalawang tungkulin na ginagampanan sa ating bansa:

Mag-patupad ng mga pagsusulit o board exam para sa lisensya ng mga nais maging isang propesyonalMangasiwa sa mga propesyonal ng ating bansa sa kanilang paggampan sa kanilang kaniya kaniyang propesyon.
