D when the energy of photons is 3.5 eV, the photoelectric current will be maximum. ​


Answer:

The correct option is A the energy of the incident photon is 4.7 eV.

Explanaption:

From Einstein photoelectric effect equation, KE(max)=hν−ϕ

⇒eV=hν−ϕ ⇒1.2=hν−3.5 ⇒hν=4.7 eVwhich is equal to theenergy of the incident photon

But, Photoelectric current depends on the intensity of radiation, not on frequency or energy of photons

.°. Hence, option (A) is the correct answer.
