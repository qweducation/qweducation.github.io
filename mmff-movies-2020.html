Explanation: The Director is the position in film production that decides how the final output should appear in cinemas. It is the second-highest position next to the producer. Every creative decision is made by the Film Director.

10) B. Cinema

Explanation: Cinema is a product of combined arts. It is an art form that started after 1884, wherein cameras were able to successfully capture motion.

11) A. Animahensayon.

Explanation: It was established in 2007 and is organized by the Animation Council of the Philippines. Animahensayon's goal is to promote and encourage Filipino Artists to produce and develop the local animation industry.

Explanation: PASI not PACI. It is one of the early animation studios in PH that has worked with Hollywood Companies like Warner Bros. Inc.

14) A. RPG Metanoia

Explanation: This is the full name of the Film: RPG Metanoia. It is the first full-length 3d animation film by Pinoys.

15) A. Advertising.

Explanation: Since ancient civilizations, advertising has been using print media. Examples can be seen in the newly excavated Thermopolium in Pompeii. A Thermopolium is the equivalent of modern fast food during the Roman Empire.
