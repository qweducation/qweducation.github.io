23. different titles of sto. nino and a short description


The different titles of Sto. Nino depends on the location and how they represent the holy child.

Here are some images of the Santo Nino:


Santo Nino de Cebu:

This is the most recognizable santo nino here in the Philippines. The image was given by the Spaniards to the queen as a gift 400 years ago and the image can be found in Cebu city.


Santo Nino de Romblon:

The image can be found in Romblon. The image has intricate details on the dress and a gold crown. The image was once stolen in 1991 and was brought back after 22 years.


Santo Nino de Molino:

The image can be found in Molino, Cavite. Every year during the feast day of the holy child, they do a 'Karakol'. It is a procession where they dance the image.


Theses are only few among the many representations of the holy child.
