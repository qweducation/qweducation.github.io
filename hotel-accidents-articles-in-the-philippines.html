The islands were part of the larger Spanish East Indies. Manila was made the capital of the Philippines in 1571. This was the time of the reign of King Philip II of Spain, whose name has remained attached to the country. Research has shown that lightning strikes during thunderstorms can cause serious injuries to people. Some of the common injuries that people may sustain during a thunderstorm include burns, cardiac arrest, neurological damage, and hearing loss.

People who have been struck by lightning during a thunderstorm may also experience psychological effects. A study published in the Journal of Nervous and Mental Disease found that lightning strike survivors often experience symptoms of post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), such as anxiety, depression, and intrusive thoughts.

It is important for people to take precautions during thunderstorms, such as seeking shelter indoors and avoiding activities such as swimming or golfing that may put them at risk of being struck by lightning.
