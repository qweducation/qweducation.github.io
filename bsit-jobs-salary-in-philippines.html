Answer:

$45000

Step-by-step explanation:

For 5 years, she earned $187,345

Then for a year (at previous job), she earns

[tex] \implies\frac{187345}{5}[/tex]

[tex]=37469[/tex]

For a year, she's earning $37,469 at previous job

Each year, Francesca earns a salary that is 2% higher

Now she earns

[tex] =\$37,469+2\%[/tex]

[tex] =\$37,469+(37,469\times2\%)[/tex]

[tex] =\$37,469+\left(37,469\times\frac{1}{5}\right)[/tex]

[tex] =\$37,469+\left(\frac{37,469}{5}\right)[/tex]

[tex] =\$37,469+7,493.8 = \bold{\$44,962.8} [/tex]

Round off to nearest thousand we get

$45000
