18. A 60-kg man climbs up to the third floor of a building which is 25 m high.What wil be the gravitational potential energy of the man as he reached the third floor?​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The gravitational potential energy of an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{GPE = mgh}[/tex]

where:

GPE = gravitational potential energy

m = mass

g = acceleration due to gravity = 9.8 m/s²

h = height

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for GPE

GPE = mgh

GPE = (60 kg)(9.8 m/s²)(25 m)

[tex]\boxed{GPE = \text{14,700 J}}[/tex]

Therefore, the gravitational potential energy of the man is 14,700 J.
