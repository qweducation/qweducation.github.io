15. Ho: Peter thinks that his crush, MJ also liked him. Which of the following denotes a Type II error? a. Peter said MJ liked him and MJ confessed that she liked Peter b. Peter said MJ does not like him but MJ confessed that she liked Peter c. Peter said MJ liked him but MJ said she does not like Peter d. Peter said MJ does not like him and MJ said she does not like Peter


Answer:

C

Step-by-step explanation:

Peter said MJ liked him, but MJ said she does not like Peter. In this case, Peter thinks that MJ likes him, but MJ denies having any romantic feelings towards Peter. This situation represents a Type II error because Peter's hypothesis (MJ likes him) is not supported by MJ's response (she does not like him), and it turns out that Peter's assumption is false.
