Answer:

Before the pandemic, there was more supply of alcohol than demand. This can be seen through shelves filled with alcohol and their low prices. However, during the first month of the lockdown, the demand exceeded the current supply and therefore caused a shortage. It was very difficult to find alcohol during that time because the production of alcohol could not catch up with the demand. But now, the demand of alcohol is near its supply due to the fact that stores have limited the number of bottles you can buy.
