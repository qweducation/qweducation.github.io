1. What are the advantages and disadvantages of summer monsoon (habagat) to our country?(Philippines)​


Answer:

Habagat brings a lot of rains and typhoons

Advantage:

1. Bring a lot of rain that helps our farmers to grow their crops especially in different areas with low supply of water

2. Rain seasons add a large amount of water in different dams that supply waters to different towns and cities

Disadvantage:

1. To much rain cause flashflood

2. To much rain affects the crops of our farmers especially in low areas that soaked in floods.

3. Heavy rains/ Typhoon destroy not only crops of our farmers but also the properties of our people.

15. What is origin of amihan and habagat​


Answer:

In the Philippines, the Summer Monsoon (West or southwest winds) is called the Habagat (ha-bag-at) and the Winter Monsoon (North or northeast winds) is called the Amihan (a-me-han). The word 'monsoon' is believed to originate from the Arabic word mawsim (season), via the Portuguese and then Dutch monsun
