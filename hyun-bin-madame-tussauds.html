what materials do i need to make my own compost bin?

Good materials are the key to good compost. Fruit and vegetable scraps, eggshells, coffee grounds, grass clippings, dry leaves, finely chopped wood, shredded paper, straw, and sawdust from untreated wood all make great compost. Bad materials can harm your pile, and eventually your garden.

where should i place my compost bin?

Compost bins are tucked away in a spare corner of the garden. Your compost bin should be easily accessible, but not in a place where occasional smells or leaching liquids will cause a nuisance. Place it on level, well-drained ground and make sure the location gets some sunlight.

13. Supposed a boy pushed a 5kg trash bin across the aisle. If the acceleration of the bin is 2.5 m/s to the left, what is the net force exerted on the trash bin?​


NET FORCE

Problem:

» Supposed a boy pushed a 5 kg trash bin across the aisle. If the acceleration of the bin is 2.5 m/s² to the left, what is the net force exerted on the trash bin?

Given:

m (mass) = 5 kg

a (acceleration) = 2.5 m/s²

F (Force) = ?

Solution:

F = m × a

= 5 kg × 2.5 m/s²

= 12.5 N

Answer:12.5 N (newtons)

VOLUME

=======================================

PROBLEM:Sandy bought a rectangular recycling bin for her office the recycling bin has the length of 7 cm a width of 10 cm and a height of 12 cm. What is the volume of the recycling bin?GIVEN:Length - 7 cmWidth - 10 cmHeight - 12 cmFORMULA:[tex]\large\sf\purple{}V=lwh[/tex]SOLUTION:[tex]\large\sf\purple{}V=lwh[/tex][tex]\large\sf\purple{}V=(7\:cm)(10\:cm)(12\:cm)[/tex][tex]\large\sf\purple{}V=(7\:cm)(120\:cm²)[/tex][tex]\large{\boxed{\sf\purple{V=840\:cm³}}}[/tex]ANSWER:The volume of the recycling bin is 840 cm³.

#CarryOnLearning

Volume Of Rectangular Prism

[tex] \underline{ \sf{Problem:}}[/tex]Sandy bought a rectangular recycling bin for her office the recycling bin has the length of 7cm a width of 10cm and a height of 12cm what is the volume of the recycling bin?

[tex] \underline{ \sf{Given:}}[/tex]Leight (L) = 7 cmWidth (W) = 10 cmHeight (H) = 12 cm

[tex] \underline{ \sf{Formula:}}[/tex]V = L × W × H

[tex] \underline{ \sf{Solution:}}[/tex]Volume = Leight × Width × HeightV = L × W × HV = 7 cm × 10 × cm × 12 cm V = 840 cm³

[tex] \underline{ \sf{Answer :}}[/tex]The volume is 840 cm³ of recycling bin.
