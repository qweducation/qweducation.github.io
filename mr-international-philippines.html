If the exchange rate is 1 Australian dollar to P26.30, how much would he have in Australian dollars?​


[tex]\huge\color{lavender}{\boxed{Answers:}}[/tex]

3. The car would cover a distance of 200 km.

4. Her husband received 17,337 pesos.

5. He would have 610.97 Australian dollars (rounded to two decimal places).

[tex]\huge\color{lavender}{\boxed{Solution:}}[/tex]

3. The speed of the car is 80 km/2 hours = 40 km/hour. Therefore, in 5 hours, the distance covered by the car would be:

distance = speed x time

distance = 40 km/hour x 5 hours

distance = 200 km

4. If the exchange rate is 1 Hong Kong dollar to P6.41, then Aling Remedios's husband would receive:

2,700 HKD x 6.41 PHP/HKD = 17,337 PHP

5. If the exchange rate is 1 Australian dollar to P26.30, then Mr. Abalos would have:

P16,043 ÷ 26.30 PHP/AUD = 610.97 AUD
