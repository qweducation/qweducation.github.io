9. ACCOUNTING: I HAVE THE ANSWER, I JUST NEED SOLUTION & SHORT EXPLANATION. 43. On December 2015, Victory Corporation, a Philippine company, entered into a 4-month forward contract to purchase $250,000 for speculative purposes. Victory's accounting year ends on December 31. Relevant exchange rates are as follows Date Spot Rate Forward Rate (to 3/31/16) December 1, 2015 P45.00 P45.50 Dec 31,2015 46.00 46.10 January 31, 2016 45.60 45.70 February 28 2016 45.40 45.55 March 31, 2016 45.10 How much exchange difference will be reported from this forward contract in 2016? A. P50,000 C. P100,000 B. P150,000 D. P250,000 ANSWER: C SOLUTION: ? EXPLANATION: ?


Answer:

pa brainliest po

Explanation:

SOLUTION:

To calculate the exchange difference, we need to compare the forward rate at the time the contract was entered into (December 1, 2015, P45.50) with the spot rate at the settlement date (March 31, 2016, P45.10).

We use the formula: Forward Rate - Spot Rate = Exchange Difference

P45.50 - P45.10 = P0.40

Then, we multiply the exchange difference by the amount of the contract in USD:

P0.40 x $250,000 = P100,000

Therefore, the exchange difference for this forward contract in 2016 is P100,000.

EXPLANATION:

The exchange difference for a forward contract is the difference between the forward rate and the spot rate at the settlement date. In this case, Victory Corporation entered into a forward contract to purchase $250,000 for speculative purposes. The relevant exchange rates were provided, and we used the formula to calculate the exchange difference.

Since the forward rate was higher than the spot rate, there was a gain for Victory Corporation. This gain of P0.40 per USD was multiplied by the amount of the contract in USD to obtain the total exchange difference of P100,000.

The exchange difference must be recognized in the income statement for the period in which it arises (in this case, 2016).

29. in 2016 , december 25 fell on a sunday. on what day of the week does christmas fall in 2025? Note that the years 2020 and 2024 are both leap years.


Answer:

Thursday

Step-by-step explanation:

Take note that after whole weeks we return to the same day. Exactly a week after today, April 21, for example which is a Tuesday, April 28 would also be a Tuesday. Hence, we determine the remainder per year.

When we divide 1 year by a week or 365 days by 7 days, we get a remainder of 1 day. That means, without a leap year involved, April 21, 2020 or today is Tuesday, and next year April 21, 2021 would be Wednesday.

Take note that leap years involve an additional day on February. We then add by 2 on that occasion. So although 2016 is also a leap year, it is not involved in the count as December 2016 comes after Feb 2016.

2016 - Sunday

2017 - Monday

2018 - Tuesday

2019 - Wednesday

2020 - Leap year (+2) Friday

2021 - Saturday

2022 - Sunday

2023 - Monday

2024 - Leap year (+2) Wednesday

2025 - Thursday

Therefore, Christmas of 2025 will happen on a Thursday.
