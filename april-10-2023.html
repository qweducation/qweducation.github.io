mean = 760 / 15

mean = 50.67

Therefore, the mean is 50.67.

To find the median, we first need to arrange the values in order from lowest to highest:

1, 3, 5, 6, 8, 9, 75, 75, 76, 80, 80, 80, 81, 81, 85

The median is the middle value. Since there are 15 values, the middle value is between the 7th and 8th value.

median = (8 + 9) / 2

median = 8.5

Therefore, the median is 8.5.
