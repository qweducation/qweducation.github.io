25. : Identify what is the type of heat transfer is shown for some common activities at home. Write your answer in your Answer Sheet or Science Activity Notebook. _______________ 1. Cooking lechon manok. _______________ 2. Ice melts because the air around is warm. _______________ 3. Drying newly washed clothes in the sun. _______________ 4. Heating the frying pan. _______________ 5. Sauna bath (tuob). _______________ 6. The cover of the cooking pot becomes hot when cooking.


Answer:

1. Radiation

2. Convection

3. Conduction

4.Conduction

5. Convection

6. Conduction

Explanation:

Radiation - this is a type of heat transfer that happens when there's a movement of electromagnetic waves between the source of heat and the food.

Convection - it is the transfer of heat through the movement of fluids - air or liquid.

Conduction - there's a transfer of heat between two objects by physical contact.

Conduction - there's a transfer of heat between two objects by physical contact (the cover and the pot itself.) The movement of air molecules inside the pot causes the heat to come in contact with the cover, hence, it can also be convection. To say that it is insulation may be wrong because the method being asked is about how the heat is transferred whereas insulation is a process of stopping or impeding the transfer of heat.
