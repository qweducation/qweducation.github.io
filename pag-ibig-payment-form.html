2. ano ang ibig sabihin ng down payment​


Answer:

A down payment is a sum of money that a buyer pays in the early stages of purchasing an expensive good or service. The down payment represents a portion of the total purchase price, and the buyer will often take out a loan to finance the remainder.

Now we take the total balance left and divide the total by 5 as the question indicates that it will be an annual pay over 5 years.

400,000 / 5 = 80,000

Now that we have the annual payment, we can then calculate for the interest for each payment made. We then take the annual payment of 80,000 and multiply it by 0.075, which is equal to 7.5%. We then add the calculated value to the annual payment of 80,000 to get the real annual payment with the 7.5% interest rate.

80,000

x0.075

6,000 + 80,000 = 86,000/year

The problem then indicates that we need to find the remaining balance after 2 years, however; we first need to calculate the total balance from the 5 annual payments.

86,000

x         5

430,000 for 5 years

Now that we have the total balance we can then subtract the payment of the 2 years.
