29. a tv set that was originally sold for 70 000 pesos was given a discount of 20% last november. in december, another 25% discount was applied to its november price. what was the selling price in december?


First, we need to find the discounted price in November.

Discounted price = base - (base × rate)
Discounted price = 70 000 - (70 000 × .20)
Discounted price = 70 000 - 14 000
Discounted price = P 56 000 - Discounted price in November.

Then...
Discounted Price = base - (base × rate)
Discounted Price = 56 000 - (56 000 × .25)
Discounted Price = 56 000 - 14 000
Discounted Price = P 42 000 - Discounted price in December.

30. A TV set was originally sold for ₱50,000 was given a discount of 20% last November. In December, another 25% discount was applied to its November price. What was the selling price in December?​


₱30,000 since the discount is 25%

Answer:

₱30000

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the original price is 50000 pesos, we multiply the discount to the original price to find out the discount for November,

₱50000 x 20% = ₱10000

The selling price should be ₱40000 since we subtract the original price to the discount for November.

In December, The original price is now ₱40000. We do the same thing in here except the discount is 25%

₱40000 x 25% = ₱10000

₱40000 - ₱10000 = ₱30000

The final answer is ₱30000.
