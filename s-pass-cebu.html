12. a Cebu Pacific plane bound for Kuala Lumpur,Malaysia accelerates on a runway from the rest to 3.22 m/s² to 32.0 s until it finally takes off . Find the distance travelled before takeoff​ answer please


Explanation:

Since we are given:

a: acceleration= 3.22 m/s2

t: time= 32.0 m/s2

vi: initial velocity= 0 m/s

And we are trying to find the change in distance Δx

We can solve:

Δx=(vi)(t) + (½)(a)(t2)

Δx= (0 m/s)(32.8 s) + (½)(3.22 m/s2)(32.8 s)2

Δx=1764.90 m

Therefore, the distance traveled before take off was 1764.90 meters.

18. Sino gumawa sa pangkat na umurong sa Kabunduoan sa Cebu?PLEASE HELP ME ITS PASSED ON THURSDAY​


Answer:

The group that retreated to the mountains in Cebu during the Philippine-American War was led by the Filipino revolutionary leader Pantaleon "Leon kilat" Villegas. Villegas was a former Spanish soldier who joined the Philippine Revolution against Spain in 1896. He later joined the Katipunan and played a key role in the Cebuano revolt against Spanish colonial rule in 1898. When the Americans arrived in Cebu in 1899, Villegas led a group of guerrilla fighters in a resistance movement against American occupation. However, Villegas was eventually betrayed by one of his own men and was assassinated in April 1898, effectively ending the resistance movement in Cebu.
