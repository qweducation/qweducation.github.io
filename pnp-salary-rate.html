10. Benjie earns 8,900.00 a month and got 534.00 as additional salary increase.What is the rate of increase?​


The rate of increase is 6%.

Step-by-Step Explanation:

Word Problem: Finding the Rate

Let us analyze the problem.

What is asked?

The problem is asking for the rate of increase in Benjie's salary.

What are the given?

Benjie's salary per month - 8,900.00

additional salary increase - 534.00

What is the formula?

rate = percentage ÷ base × 100

To find the rate of increase in Benjie's salary, you have to divide the percentage by the base. The percentage refers to the part of the base or the whole determined by the rate and the base refers to the original amount or the whole. Then, the rate refers to the ratio of the percentage to the base. It is the number written with the percent symbol (%).

In the problem, the percentage is the salary increase which is 534.00 and the base is the salary per month which is 8,900.00. Plug in the values in the formula.

What is the solution?

rate = percentage ÷ base × 100

= 534.00 ÷ 8,900.00 × 100

= 0.06 × 100

= 6%

What is the final answer?

The rate of increase is 6%.
