5. review and revise helps you in exam?​


Answer:

In examinations, revision will help you be better able to answer test questions. You'll feel prepared. And the security and confidence that comes from that feeling, from the knowledge that you've revised what you needed to,will reduce test anxiety - a necessary step to sitting an exam well.

Review sessions can thus serve to help clarify questions about the materials/notes, make students feel more confident about possible exam material, and provide a valuable metacognitive opportunity to examine what they know and do not know. Well-designed review sessions help students organize the material to be studied.

25. Essay for Nutrition Month Theme 2017 (English and Tagalog)


ENGLISH
The Nutrition Month theme 2017: “Healthy Diet Gawing Habit for Life”


This 2017, our theme aims to let us understand what we need to do. In this theme “Health Diet Gawing Habit for Life”, we have to promote awareness on malnutrition, obesity, and other related health issues. We need to encourage everyone that we have to consume healthy foods instead of unhealthy ones.


As most of us know, obesity affects not just appearance but disease processes as well. With healthier diet, our bodies can better shield disease-causing viruses and bacteria. Healthy diet is a way of protecting and preventing people from diseases, maintaining and providing our body with essential nutrients that it needs.


Also, the theme makes a call to the farmers, producers and manufacturers to produce healthier, organic foods instead of producing products that are filled with harmful chemicals and preservatives.


Most importantly, the theme emphasizes that real change can only be seen through repetitive actions. This means that in order for us to achieve this healthy goal, we make it a habit of eating healthy and producing healthier foods. Habit means that we have to make it an everyday routine and not just a one-time event.

TAGALOG
Ang tema ng Nutrition Month 2017: "Healthy Diet Making Habit for Life"

Ang tema ay naglalayong ipaalam sa mga tao na maunawaan kung ano ang kailangang gawin. Sa temang ito na "Healthy Diet Gawing Habit for Life", kailangan nating itaguyod ang kamalayan sa malnutrisyon, labis na katabaan, at iba pang kaugnay na mga isyu sa kalusugan. Kailangan nating hikayatin ang lahat na kinakailangang kumonsumo tayo ng malusog na pagkain sa halip na mga hindi malusog.


Sa pagkakaalam ng karamihan, ang labis na katabaan ay nakakaapekto hindi lamang sa hitsura kung hindi pati na rin sa mga proseso ng sakit. Sa malusog na diyeta, ang ating mga katawan ay maaaring mas mahusay na kalasag ng mga virus at bakterya na nagdudulot ng sakit. Ang malusog na diyeta ay isang paraan ng pagprotekta at pagpigil sa mga tao mula sa sakit, pagpapanatili at pagbibigay sa ating katawan ng mahahalagang sustansiya na kailangan nito.


Gayundin, ang tema ay tumawag sa mga magsasaka, producer at mga tagagawa upang makagawa ng malusog, organic na pagkain sa halip ng paggawa ng mga produkto na puno ng mga mapanganib na kemikal at preserbatiba.


Pinakamahalaga sa lahat, ang tema ay nagpapahiwatig na ang tunay na pagbabago ay makikita lamang sa pamamagitan ng mga paulit-ulit na pagkilos. Nangangahulugan ito na upang makamit natin ang malusog na layunin na ito, dapat nating gawing araw-araw ang pagkonsumo ng mga makapagpapalusog na pagkain. Kailangan nating gawin itong pang-araw-araw na gawain at hindi lamang isang pangyayari.
