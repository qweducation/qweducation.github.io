27. the population of rats in a farm at the beginning of a thousand 18 was 516 the population has been steadily decreasing by 4% per year find the population in the beginning of year 2022​


Step-by-step explanation:

The population of rats in a farm at the beginning of 2018 was 516 the population has been steadily decreasing by 4% per year. Find the population in the beginning of year 2022.

In the beginning of 2018, there were 516 rats and every year, its population decreases by 4 percent.

In 2019, it will have

[tex]516 - 4\% = \\ 516 - 20.64 = \\ 495.36[/tex]

In 2020, it will have

[tex]495.36 - 4\% = \\ 495.36 - 19.8144 = \\ 475.5456[/tex]

In 2021, it will have

[tex]475.5456 - 4\% = \\ 475.5456 - 19.021824 = \\ 456.523776[/tex]

In 2022, it will have

[tex]456.523776 - 4\% = \\ 456.523776 - 18.26095104 = \\ 438.2628249[/tex]

Therefore, the rat population in 2022 will be 438.2628249
