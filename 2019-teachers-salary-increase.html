1. What was the salary of Alliona in 2017 if her salary in 2019 was PHP 20,000.00 with an increase of 40% annually?​


Answer:

Alliona's salary in 2017 was Php 10,204.00.

Step-by-step explanation:

a₁   ⇒  2017 ⇒  Unknown

a₂  ⇒ 2018  

an = a₃  ⇒ 2019  ⇒ Php20,000;  n = 3

Annual rate of increase: 40%  or 0.4

Common ratio, r  = 1+0.4  or  r = 1.4 or  1 ⁴/₁₀

r (as fraction) =   1 ²/₅  or 7/5

Geometric sequence:

an = a₁ (r)ⁿ⁻¹

Find the salary in 2017, a₁:

20,000 = a₁ (7/5)³⁻¹

20,000 = a₁ (7/5)²

20,000 = a₁(49/25)

a₁ = 20,000 (25/49)

a₁ = 10,204.08

a₁ = 10,204

Alliona's salary in 2017 was Php10,204.00.

Check:

Rate of increase 40%

2017 = 10,204

2018 = 10,204 × 1.4 = 14,285.60

2019 = 14,285.60 × 1.4 = 19,999.84  or Php 20,000  (True)

17. which would be priorities more classroom or salary increase of teachers pls answer on your own opinion


Answer:

Both classroom resources and teacher salaries are essential to the quality of education provided to students, and they are not mutually exclusive. However, if we have to prioritize one over the other, providing adequate classroom resources should be the priority. Classroom resources such as textbooks, technology, equipment, and other supplies directly impact the learning environment and student outcomes. When students have access to quality resources, they are more likely to engage in their learning, participate in class, and perform better academically.

While teacher salaries are important in attracting and retaining qualified teachers, it is not always the determining factor for effective teaching. Teachers who are passionate about their profession and who have access to quality resources can make a significant impact on their students' learning, regardless of their salaries. However, fair and competitive salaries are essential to retaining and motivating teachers, and it is crucial to compensate them adequately for their hard work and dedication.
