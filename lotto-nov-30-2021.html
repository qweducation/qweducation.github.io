Assuming same formula to be used in the screenshot.

n= 30 (30 days of November)

A1= 10 PhP (starting term)

[tex]sn = \frac{n}{2} (a1 + an)[/tex]

[tex]s30 = \frac{30}{2} (10 + 300)[/tex]
