15. summarize the sample news report three to five sentences news report-deped sets opening of school year 2022-2023 on August 22​


Answer:

The Department of Education (DepEd) on Friday said it is ready for the scheduled opening of classes on Aug. 22.

"All systems go na tayo for the opening of classes," DepEd spokesperson Michael Poa said in a press conference.

Poa said a total of 24,175 public and private schools nationwide or 46 percent will implement five-day face-to-face classes, while 29,721 schools or about 51.8 percent will implement blended learning modality.

Meanwhile, the remaining 1.29 percent or about 1,004 schools will conduct distance learning.

Poa, citing Aug. 19 DepEd data, said the current total enrollment on the basic education level is 27 million learners, which is 96 percent of the agency's target of 28.6 million.

DepEd Undersecretary Epimaco Densing III earlier said schools will be given options of in-person, blended, or full-distance learning only until Oct. 31.

After this, all enrolled students will be required to attend face-to-face classes, under DepEd Order (DO) 034, series of 2022.

"This is mandatory, they are all required to go to school and the mandate is really to eventually go face-to-face. Everybody who's enrolled should abide by the Department Order, parents are also encouraged to ensure that their students go to school. Again, we're trying to do some educational recovery," he said.

According to the same DO 034, there are 203 days for Academic Year 2022-2023 for public schools, which will begin on Aug. 22, 2022 and will end on July 7, 2023.

17. any reflection about"DepEd SET TO CONDUCT THE BASIC EDUCATION REPORT (BER) 2023 ON JANUARY 30"​


Answer:

The Department of Education (DepEd) conducting the Basic Education Report (BER) 2023 on January 30 is a positive step towards evaluating the progress of the basic education system in the Philippines. The BER will provide valuable insights into the state of education, including the enrollment rates, graduation rates, and other important indicators. This information will be used to identify areas of strength and areas that need improvement, allowing the government to make informed decisions on how to allocate resources and support the development of the education system.

Moreover, the BER will provide an opportunity for stakeholders, including teachers, students, parents, and the community, to have a voice in the process of evaluating the education system. It will also serve as a platform for discussion and collaboration among all parties involved, promoting transparency and accountability in the implementation of education policies and programs.

In conclusion, the BER is an important tool for monitoring the progress of basic education in the Philippines and for ensuring that all children have access to quality education. The results of the BER will be critical in guiding future decision-making and shaping the future of education in the country.

Answer:

However, here are some possible advantages that some people or experts may point out:
