The two grade six girls were asked to do the following: Girl A will be go upstairs from first floor to third floor. Girl B will be go downstairs from third floor to first floor. Both of them were given same time to do the task simultaneously. 1. Which of them finished first? Why?​


Answer:

Girl A, who went upstairs from the first floor to the third floor, finished first. This is because it requires less effort to go up stairs than to go down stairs. When going up stairs, gravity is working with the person, making it easier to ascend. On the other hand, when going down stairs, gravity is working against the person, making it harder to descend. Therefore, Girl A had an advantage in this task and finished first.
