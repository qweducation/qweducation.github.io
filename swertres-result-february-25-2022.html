13. What is the simplified ratio of the number of days in a week to the number of days in a month of February 2022?


Answer:

7:1

Step-by-step explanation:

Question:

Bookmark

Find the ratio of the given statement in the simplest form.

3 weeks to 3 days.

Solution:

We know that,

1week=7days.

So,

3weeks:3days = 3×7days:3days

= 21days:3days

= 21/3

= 7/1

= 7:1
