Answer:

84 marks

Step-by-step explanation:

total score : 100

total number of questions : 25

thus, score of one question : 100÷25 = 4

number of questions skipped : 4

score of skipped questions : 4×4 = 16

thus, highest possible score = 100 - 16 = 84

29. Liza is skip counting by 6 by saying 5 on the first skip then 11 on the second skip and so on.what number does ahe say on the 11th skip?


Answer:

71

Step-by-step explanation:

We can say that this is an arithmetic sequence with a common difference (d) of 6 and first term (a1) of 5.

Now, we need to find the 12th term.

a12 = a1 + (n - 1)d

a12 = 5 + (12 - 1)(6)

a12 = 5 + (11)(6)

a12 = 5 + 66

a12 = 71
