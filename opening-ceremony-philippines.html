28. make a reflection on the following filipino rituals and ceremony covering all regions of the Philippines ​


Filipino rituals and ceremony covering all regions of the Philippines:

1. Arrhae

2. Wedding Rope

3. Throwing rice grains

4. Pamamanhikan

EXPLANATION:

1. Arrhae

The arrhae ceremony or often known as the cedar is a celebration that originates from Spanish culture and Catholicism. In this ceremony, the bride will receive a small basket from the groom.

2. Wedding Rope

In the implementation of the wedding reception, there is one interesting procession. Filipinos usually attach a rope that will tie the wrists or waists of the bride and groom.

3. Throwing rice grains

Throwing rice grains to the bride and groom is one of the ceremonies carried out by the people of the Philippines. The goal is to pray for the fertility of the couple so that they are able to give birth to many offspring in the future.

4. Pamamanhikan

Pamamanhikan is an important ceremony for the families of both parties of the bride and groom. This ceremony will be held by the women's side and attended by the families of the men with Filipino specialties. The pamamanhikan ceremony symbolizes the act of respecting and asking for blessings from the parents of both parties of the bride and groom. In addition, it can also be used as an opportunity to plan a wedding to be held.
