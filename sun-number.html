25. the sun of two numbers is 17. their difference is 3. find the number.​


Answer:

10 and 7

Step-by-step explanation:

let the first number = x, second number = y and assume x > y


x + y = 17 (Equation 1)

x - y = 3 (Equation 2)

Add Equation 1 and Equation 2.

2x = 20

2x/2 = 20/2

x = 10

Substitute x to Equation 1.

10 + y = 17

y = 17 - 10

y = 7

Answer:

10 and 7

Step-by-step explanation:

Hence, the two required numbers are 10 and 7 .
