TLE

1. Identify some of the practices you and your family experience during the Christmas season.

One of the practices my family and I experience during the Christmas season is attending Simbang Gabi or Misa de Gallo, which is a nine-day novena mass that starts on December 16 and culminates on Christmas Eve. We also put up Christmas decorations like parols (star-shaped lanterns), Christmas lights, and belens (nativity scenes). On Christmas day, we usually have a big feast with traditional Filipino dishes like lechon (roasted pig), hamon (ham), and bibingka (rice cake).

2. How similar or different is your celebration or practice from those of your friend/ classmate? In what ways are they the same? In what ways are they different?

My celebration and practices during Christmas are quite similar to those of my friends/classmates. We also attend Simbang Gabi, put up Christmas decorations, and have a big feast on Christmas day. However, there may be differences in the specific dishes we prepare or the way we decorate our homes.
