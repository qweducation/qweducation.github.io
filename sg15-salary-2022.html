28. Karl had his salary reduced by 10%.He was later promoted and his salary was increased by 10%. if his original salary was $ 20 000, What is his present salary?


First, we have to find out 10% of his orginal salary which is $2000.

Then we have to subtract it from $20000 which will be $18000.

Then we will increase it by 10% and 10% of $18000 is $1800 wich we will add to his salary in a total of $19,800.


Please mark brainliest or thanks if it helped!

  Original  salary  =     $20,000

   reduced by 10%       =   20,000 * 0.1  =   2,000

    interim salary          =     20,000 - 2000  =   18,000

    increased by 10%    =      18000* 0.1    =   1,800

     so the present salary     =   18,000 + 1,800

                                              =   $19,800
