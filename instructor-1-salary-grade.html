22. By mistake, an instructor has erased the grade that one of ten students in her class received in a final examination. However, she knows that the students averaged is 71 on the examination, and that the other nine students received grades of 96, 44, 82, 70, 47, 74, 94, 78, and 56. What must have been the grade that the teacher erased?​


Answer: 69

Step-by-step explanation:

* The formula for the average of ungrouped data is average = (x1 + x2 + x3 + ... + xn)/(the number of data)

*Let x be the unknown number (erased grade)

Solution:

average = (x1 + x2 + x3 + ... + xn)/(the number of data)

71 = (96 + 44 + 82 + 70+ 47 + 74 + 94 + 78 + 56 + x)/10

710 = 641 + x

x = 710 - 641

x = 69
