﻿Ang bansang Espanya ay isa sa mga pangunahing bansa na nagpasimula ng kolonyalismo sa buong daigdig. Nasakop nila ang ating bansang Pilipinas nang mahigit tatlong daang taon.

Compute for the mean expenses of the XYZ Corporation given the following: Name Total Sales Total Expenses Quezon City14,950.004,933.50 Caloocan City18,290.006,035.70 Marikina City37,200.0012,276.00 Cebu City18,900.006,237.00 Davao City45,000.0014,850.00 Mandaluyong City 23,000.007,590.00 Cavite22,000.007,260.00 Laguna21,000.006,930.00 Manila66,000.0021,780.00 Iloilo34,000.0011,220.00


Answer:

The mean expenses of the XYZ Corporation is 9,911.22.

Further explanation:

In statistics, there are three ways to measure the central tendency or the numbers that indicates the center of a data. And these are the following:

Mean – Also called as average. It is the value after adding up all the given numbers then dividing it by the count of numbers in a data.Median – It is the middle number in an ascending list of numbers. If the count of numbers is even, then the median will be the mean/ average of the two middle numbers.  Mode – It is the number that have been repeated the most. The mode does not exist when no number is repeated in the given data.  

Solution for the XYZ Corporation’s mean expenses:Given:  

No Name                         Total Sales    Total Expenses  

1 Quezon City                  14,950.00     4,933.50  

2 Caloocan City               18,290.00     6,035.70  

3 Marikina City                 37,200.00    12,276.00  

4 Cebu City                      18,900.00     6,237.00  

5 Davao City                    45,000.00    14,850.00  

6 Mandaluyong City        23,000.00    7,590.00  

7 Cavite                            22,000.00    7,260.00  

8 Laguna                          21,000.00     6,930.00  

9 Manila                           66,000.00    21,780.00  

10 Iloilo                              34,000.00    11,220.00  

                              TOTAL  300,340.00   99,112.20

Solution:Get the sum/ total of all the expenses in all 10 locations: Quezon City, Caloocan City, Marikina City, Cebu City, Davao City, Mandaluyong City, Cavite, Laguna, Manila, and Iloilo which is 99,112.20. Then divide the total expense with the count of all the given data.  

       Mean = SUM divided by COUNT

       Mean = 99,112.20/ 10

       Mean = 9,911.22
