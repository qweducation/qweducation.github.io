22. what is the gain of 1000 pesos？​


Answer:

A gain of 1,000 pesos is translated to positive integer, +1,000.

Step-by-step explanation:

The integers or signed numbers are represented in real-life situation.

Positive signs represent increase or gain in values.  Increasing values can be to the right, up, above, north and east.

Negative signs represent decrease or loss in values.  Decreasing values can be to the left, under, below, south, and west.

To lose is a negative integer.  Its opposite, to gain, is a positive integer.

Therefore, a gain of 1,000 pesos is +1,000.
