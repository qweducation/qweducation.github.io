x = Rey's earnings
y = Luis's earnings
z = Dennis' earnings

Given:
x + y + z = 72,000 eq. 1
6000 + x = z eq. 2
9000 + y = z eq. 3


6000 + x = z
x = z - 6000 eq. 4

9000 + y = z
y = z - 9000 eq. 5

Substitute the eq. 4 and 5 to eq. 1
x + y + z = 72,000
(z - 6000) + (z - 9000) + z = 72,000
z - 6000 + z - 9000 + z = 72,000
z + z + z - 6000 - 9000 = 72,000
3z - 15000 = 72,000
3z = 72,000 + 15,000
3z = 87,000
3z/3 = 87,000/3
z = 29,000

Therefore, the ANSWER is 29,000. It is the money earned by Dennis.

29. ・1. During the athletic meet, it took Dennis 5 minutes to swim back and forth the pool while kurim mave it in 3/2 minutes. How many minutes behind Kurim was Dennis ?​


Answer:

To find out how many minutes behind Kurim was Dennis, you need to divide Kurim's time by the distance he swam and then multiply that number by the distance Dennis swam. That will give you Kurim's time for the same distance Dennis swam. You can then subtract that number from Dennis's time to find out how many minutes behind Kurim he was.

Here's the calculation:

(3/2 minutes) / (back and forth the pool) * (back and forth the pool) = 3/2 minutes

5 minutes - (3/2 minutes) = 5 minutes - 3/2 minutes = 15/2 minutes = 7 1/2 minutes

So, Dennis was 7 1/2 minutes behind Kurim.
