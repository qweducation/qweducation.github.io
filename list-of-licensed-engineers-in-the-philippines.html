6. explain how a tourist could obtain a driver's license in the Philippines ​


Explanation:

Short version

As a foreigner, you can drive in the Philippines up to 90 days since arrival, with your foreign driver's license and passport. Mind you, an international driver's permit (IDP) is a permit only valid if the license is valid too, and thus will not help extend this duration as very well explained here. So, after those initial 90 days, you need to get a Philippine driver's license based on your foreign driver's license. This requires a visa valid for one (1) year. However, one year visa's are not given to foreigners, only extensions for one or two months.

22. In the philippines, there are two licensing boards which an aspiring counselor may choose to take. research on the guidelines and qualifications needed on each. compare and differentiate the two.


Answer:

There are two licensing boards in the Philippines that an aspiring counselor may choose to take. These are: The Professional Regulatory Board of Psychology (PRB), and the Philippine Association of Clinical Psychologists (PACP).

The PRB requires a bachelor's degree, plus one year of clinical work experience. Candidates must also pass the Psychology Licensure Examination, as well as a written and oral examination before being issued a license.

The PACP requires a bachelor's degree, plus two years of clinical work experience. Candidates must pass an oral examination before being issued a license.
