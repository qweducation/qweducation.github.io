10. anna has an initial investment of 280000 in a bank. every month she withdraw 15000 to run her small business . how long will the money be used up completely given the rest of withdrawal?


Answer: 18.67 months or 1 year, 6 months, and 21 days.

Given:

Investment: 280,000
Withdrawal/month:15,000
How long will the money be used up completely given the rest of withdrawal?

Solution:

Let;
X=duration of money be used up

x=investment/withdrawal
x=280,000/15,000
x=18.67 months

Since 1 year=12 months, 1 month=30 days

x=18.67months (1year/12 months)
x=1.56 years

Convert .56 year into months:

12months/year x 0.56 year=6.72 months

Convert .72 months into days

0.72 month ( 30 days/month)=21 days

Therefore:
x = 18.67 months or 1 year, 6 months, and 21 days.
