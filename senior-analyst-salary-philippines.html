1. The salary of a junior auditor is 5/8 of the salary of a senior auditor. If a senior auditor earns 45 000.00 pesos a month, how much is the difference between their salaries


Answer:

The difference between the salaries is Php 16,875.00.

Step-by-step explanation:

Salary of senior auditor:

45,000.00

Salary of junior auditor:

(5/8)(45,000.00) = 28,125.00

Difference between their salaries:

45,000.00 - 28,125.00 = 16,875.00

The difference between the salaries is Php 16,875.00.

18. why are blue collar workers in the Philippines not given decent salaries?


Answer:

Wages are low because there are many job seekers and a few job creators. It is a matter of supply and demand. This allows Filipino businesses to keep wages low for blue collar workers since there is no competitive pressure from foreign majority owned businesses for job seekers.
