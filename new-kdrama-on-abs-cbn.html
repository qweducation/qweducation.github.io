Currently, GMA stands grateful and strong after the AlDub phenomena which rose to fame shunning Showtime back then.

In conclusion:

ABS-CBN is an essential choice for the typical viewer that enjoys drama, comedy, etc. GMA-7 is more reliant when it comes to news and they also have Eat Bulaga! which is a famous noontime show.
