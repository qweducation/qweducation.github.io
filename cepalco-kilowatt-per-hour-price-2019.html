28. An electric range uses 12,200 watts per hour and is run an average of 60 hours a year. How many kilowatt-hour is this?


Answer:

First, we need to calculate the total amount of energy used by the electric range in a year:

12,200 watts per hour x 60 hours per year = 732,000 watt-hours per year

To convert watt-hours to kilowatt-hours, we need to divide by 1000:

732,000 watt-hours per year ÷ 1000 = 732 kilowatt-hours per year

Therefore, the electric range uses 732 kilowatt-hours per year.
