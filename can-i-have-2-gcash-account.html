Cash in Bank: 1000

Withdrawal: half of 500

Total Cash: X

X = Cash on Hand + [Cash in Bank - ([tex]\frac{500}{2}[/tex])]

X = 1000 +  [1000 - ([tex]\frac{500}{2}[/tex])]

X = 1000 +  [1000 - 250]

X = 1000 + 750

X= 1750
