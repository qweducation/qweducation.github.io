25. A raise of 15% brings P12,500 a month to Nick’s salary. What was his basic salary a month?


Answer:

His new basic salary would be Php 14,375.00

Step-by-step explanation:

STEP 1: List down all the given

A raise of 15% brings P12,500 a month to Nick’s salary. What was his basic salary a month?

Raise of 15% which is the rate.

P12,500 a month salary which is the base.

What was his basic salary a month?

STEP 2: Use the formula of percentage - rate - base.

Since both rate and base is present, we will find the percentage.

[tex]P=RB[/tex] where in P is the percentage, R is the rate, and B is the base.

We will use this formula to find the increased amount.

STEP 3: Substitute.

Increased amount:

[tex]P=(12500)(0.15)[/tex]

[tex]P=1,875.00[/tex]

Basic Salary + increased amount:

[tex]Salary=12,500.00+1,875.00[/tex]

[tex]Salary =14,375[/tex]
