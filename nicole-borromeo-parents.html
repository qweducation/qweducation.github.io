14. Nicole earns 30,000 php a month from selling baked goods. She saves 1/5 of it and gives 1/2 of what was left to her parents. How much money was left for Nicole to spend for herself?


Answer: P12,000.00

Step-by-step explanation:

First find the 1/5 of 30,000

30,000 x 1/5 = P6,000.00 (the money she saves)

Then we subtract 6,000 from 30,000 in order to obtain the money left

30,000 - 6,000 = P24,000.00 (money left)

Now, we'll find the half of the 24,000

24,000 / 2 = P12,000.00 (amount she gives to her parents)

Since she saves the 6,000 and gives her parents 12,000, we'll subtract them from 30,000 to find what will be the money left for Nicole to spend

30,000 - 12,000 - 6,000 = P12,000.00 (money left for herself)

28. What is the meaning of the poem "Restless" by Cecilia Borromeo?​


Answer:

Structure:  The poem’s structure consists with three stanzas with four lines each. The poem does not follow any rhyme scheme and the theme seems to be sad most of the time.

Scheme: The poem is written in free verse, meaning there is no restriction of form on how the poem was constructed. On the other hand, the author is able to express what was in her thoughts; that she somehow wanted attention from all of the hard work she had encountered.

Figurative Language: The poem is mostly consisted with metaphors, as to describe an idea that the author would like to say to the reader. The writing style and the words also included personification, as it brings the objects to life to resemble her history. They are also traces of simile within poem.
