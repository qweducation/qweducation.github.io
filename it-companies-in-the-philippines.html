18. health insurance companies in the philippines


Answer:

HMO and Health Insurance

Explanation:

There are two kinds of health insurance providers in the Philippines. We have HMOs or health maintenance organizations where they provide coverage for outpatient, in-patient and emergency services. The top companies are Intellicare, Maxicare, ValuCare, MediCard and PhilCare. The way it works is that you pay a premium per year and if you get sick within the year, you could get coverage. Usually pre-existing condition are not covered, depending on the rules of the company. If you do not use the services for the year, no money back is to be expected.

The other type is the investment-lined health insurance where investment companies provide health benefits to those who subscribe to their funds. The top companies are SunLife, Manulife, Axa, PruLife and Insular Health. What they do is they provide coverage for dreaded diseases. If you are diagnosed with any of the dreaded diseases, a set amount is given to you for medical purposes.

Another rising type of medical insurance is the prepaid insurance. This is where people could purchase health insurance by "tingi". There are packages that only cater to outpatient, laboratory or dental procedures.
