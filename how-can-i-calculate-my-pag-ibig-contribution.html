CITATION STYLE

I may contribute to the welfare of the proper citation of my own research by looking for trustworthy sources that I may use in my paper. Additionally, I must cite sources according to the American Psychological Association (APA) style to make sure that I am properly acknowledging the sources of the concepts I am employing in my research. To avoid being accused of plagiarism, I must cite any ideas or research that I borrow or known as citation.

EXAMPLE OF CITATION

According to Cruz (2022) every time we use a quote or material from a source in scholarly work, always must include a brief acknowledgement called an in-text citation. It identifies the source so that the reader can learn where you acquired your knowledge.

• APA is the most common citation style used by many researches.

1. APA ( American Psychological Association)

2. MLA ( Modern Language Association)
