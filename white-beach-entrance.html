30. Me and my friend go to the beach the entrance fee is ₱50 per people and we rent a cottage for ₱500.How are we if the total pay is ₱800?​


Answer:

If the entrance fee is ₱50 per person and you and your friend went to the beach, you paid ₱50 x 2 = ₱100 for the entrance fee.

And if you rent a cottage for ₱500, the total amount you paid for the cottage and entrance fee is ₱100 + ₱500 = ₱600.

If the total amount you paid is ₱800, it means you still have ₱800 - ₱600 = ₱200 left. You have an extra ₱200 to spend.
