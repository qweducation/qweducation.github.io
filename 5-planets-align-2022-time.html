2. In the month of February, several planets are visible in the night sky. These include:

Venus: Known as the "morning star" or "evening star," Venus is currently visible in the early morning hours just before sunrise.

Mars: Mars is visible in the early evening hours, appearing as a bright reddish-orange dot in the sky.

Jupiter: Jupiter is also visible in the evening hours, shining brightly in the southeastern sky.

Saturn: Saturn rises in the late evening and is visible for much of the night.
