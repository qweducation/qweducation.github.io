Answer:

Republic Act No. 7687, also known as the “Science and Technology Scholarship Act of 1994”, provides for scholarships to talented and deserving students whose families' socio-economic status do not exceed the set cut-off values of certain indicators.

15% of 850 is 127.5

It means that only 127.5 of those who took the scholarship are the ones who achieved the the Department of Science & Technology's standards and will pass the scholarship. While 722.5 other applicants won't be able to make it in.
