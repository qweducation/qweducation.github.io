1. Jack purchased a computer using a payment plan offered by the store. The conditions were stated, Cash Price = $2300, or 20% down payment plus 12 equal payments of $160 each”. Calculate a) the deposit b) the total monthly payments c) the total cost of the computer on the payment plan


Answer:

A) Deposits - $460

B) Total monthly payments - $1920

C) Total cost of computer - $2380

Step by Step explainations:

a) The deposit, is 20% of the cash price. Given that the cash price is $2300, the deposit can be calculated as:

Deposit = 20% of $2300

Deposit = 0.20 * $2300

Deposit = $460

b) The total monthly payments are given as $160 each, and there are 12 equal monthly payments. Therefore, the total monthly payments can be calculated as:

Total Monthly Payments = Monthly Payment * Number of Payments

Total Monthly Payments = $160 * 12

Total Monthly Payments = $1920

c) The total cost of the computer on the payment plan is the sum of the deposit and the total monthly payments. Therefore, the total cost of the computer on the payment plan can be calculated as:

Total Cost of Computer = Deposit + Total Monthly Payments

Total Cost of Computer = $460 + $1920

Total Cost of Computer = $2380
