Answer:

1. CELLPHONE = 47.8 cm

2. 20 PESO BILL = 45.2 cm

3. HANDKERCHIEF = 152 cm

4. SHORT BONDPAPER = 99 cm

5. INTERMEDIATE PAD PAPER = 90 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

1. CELLPHONE:

Length= 16.4 cm

Width= 7.5 cm

Perimeter

= 2L + 2W

= 2 (16.4 cm) + 2 (7.5 cm)

= 32.8 cm + 15 cm

= 47.8 cm

2. 20 PESO BILL:

Length= 16 cm

Width= 6.6 cm

Perimeter

= 2L + 2W

= 2 (16 cm) + 2 (6.6 cm)

= 32 cm + 13.2 cm

= 45.2 cm

3. HANDKERCHIEF:

Length= 38 cm

Perimeter

= 4S

= 4 (38 cm)

= 152 cm

4. SHORT BONDPAPER:

Length= 27.9 cm

Width= 21.6 cm

Perimeter

= 2L + 2W

= 2 (27.9 cm) + 2 (21.6 cm)

= 55.8 cm + 43.2 cm

= 99 cm

5. INTERMEDIATE PAD PAPER:

Length= 25 cm

Width= 20 cm

Perimeter

= 2L + 2W

= 2 (25 cm) + 2 (20 cm)

= 50 cm + 40 cm

= 90 cm
