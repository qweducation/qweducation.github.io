Let X be Janna's age and Y be Jelo's age.

We know that X + Y = 40 and X - 2 = 2Y

Substituting the second equation into the first equation, we get X + Y = 40

[tex]X - 2 + Y = 2Y \\ 3Y = 40 - 2 \\ Y = 14[/tex]

Substituting this value back into X - 2 = 2Y, we get X - 2 = 2 * 14

X = 30

Thus, Janna's age is X = 30 years.
