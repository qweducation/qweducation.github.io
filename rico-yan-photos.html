4. if Rico is waiting 50m straight forward and the suddenly stops after 1 min, compute the speed of Rico while working?​


SPEED

Problem: If Rico is waiting 50 m straight forward and the suddenly stops after 1 min. Compute the speed of Rico while working?

Answer: Rico's speed while working is 50 m/min.

[tex] \Large\bold{SOLUTION:} [/tex]

To calculate the speed, we must use the formula below. All we have to do just to find the speed is to use division and divide distance with time.

[tex]\bold{Formula : } \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \\ \boxed{ \sf \: Speed = \dfrac{Distance}{Time} \:}[/tex]

Given values:

Distance = 50 metersTime = 1 minute

Required:

Speed = Unknown

Substitute the given values into the formula and solve for speed:

[tex]\sf:\implies Speed = \dfrac{Distance}{Time}[/tex]

[tex]\sf:\implies Speed = \dfrac{50 \: meters}{1 \: minute}[/tex]

[tex]\sf:\implies \bold{Speed = \underline{\boxed{ \bold{50 \: m/min}}}} \\[/tex]

Therefore, the Rico's speed while working is 50 m/min.
