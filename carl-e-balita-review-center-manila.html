7. What is the ratio of the ages of Annie and Carl is 12:15. Anne is 72 yrs.old. How old is Carl?


Answer: 90

(72÷12) 15 = 90

6 x 15 = 90

ANG SAGOT!

Makatutulong ang paggamit ng proportion sa problem na ito.

Pero ano muna itong proportion?

Eun eung dalawang fraction na napagigitnaan ng equal sign = na nagsasabing magkaparehas lamang ang fractions na eun.

Katulad na lang ng:

[tex] \frac{1}{2} = \frac{2}{4} [/tex]

Ngayon, gamitin na natin, ha?

[tex] \frac{12}{15} = \frac{72}{n} [/tex]

Sa pag-convert ng ratio to fraction, gagawin mo lang na numerator ang unang number at denominator ang pangalawa.

Then eung n ay eung hinahanap nating sagot na kung saan ay edad ni Carl ngayon.

I-solve na natin sa tulong ni cross-multiplication:

[tex]12n = (15)(72) \\ \frac{12n}{12} = \frac{1080}{12} \\ n = 90[/tex]

Ayan, ang edad ni Carl ay 90 years old.

10. ten years ago, sam was s years old and carl was thrice as old as sam. how.old carl now?


Since no values are given,  we solve the Literal Equation.

Present ages:
Sam:  s  years old
Carl:  c years old

Ages 10 years ago:
Sam:  s - 10
Carl: c - 10

Ten years ago:
Carl's age = 3 (Sam's age)
c - 10 = 3 (s-10)
c = 3s - 30 + 10
c = 3s - 20

Find Sam's age at present:

c = 3s - 20
3s = c + 20
3s/3 = c/3 + 20/3

s = c/3 + 20/3    Literal Equation of Sam's age at present
