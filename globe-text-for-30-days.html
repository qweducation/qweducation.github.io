14. locate the United States and Australia on a Globe. explain why in Januay, the united states experience cold, winter days while Australia experience hot, Summer days.​


INSTRUCTIONS:

Locate the United States and Australia on a Globe. Explain why in January, the united states experience cold, winter days while Australia experience hot, summer days.

ANSWER:

⇛ The United States and Australia are located on opposite hemispheres of the globe. The United States is situated primarily in the Northern Hemisphere between the equator and the North Pole, while Australia is located in the Southern Hemisphere between the equator and the South Pole. In January, the United States is experiencing its winter season because the Earth's axis is tilted away from the sun in the Northern Hemisphere during this time. This means that the Northern Hemisphere receives less direct sunlight, resulting in cooler temperatures. Furthermore, the proximity of the United States to the North Pole also contributes to cold winter weather. Meanwhile, Australia is experiencing its summer season in January. In the Southern Hemisphere, the Earth's axis is tilted towards the sun during this time, resulting in more direct sunlight and warmer temperatures. The closer proximity of Australia to the equator also contributes to hot summer weather. Overall, the location of the United States and Australia on opposite hemispheres of the globe, as well as the Earth's axial tilt, contribute to the differences in climate and seasonal variations experienced by both countries during the month of January.
