17. Orly invested ₱100 000.00 to an account that pays a simple interest of 3%annually. Find the interest earned after 2 years


Answer:

The interest earned is Php 6,000.

Step-by-step explanation:

Simple Interest Formula:

Interest = Principal × Rate × Time

Given:

Principal:  Php  100,000Interest Rate:   3%  or  .03Time:  2 yearsInterest earned (simple):  I

Find the amount of interest, I:

I = (100,000) × (.03) × (2)

I = 6,000

The interest earned is Php 6,000.
