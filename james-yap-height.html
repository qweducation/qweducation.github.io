7. James yap runs fast while dribbling the ball. as he crosses the middle part of the basketball court, he changes his phase to avoid his opponent. what specific component of physical fitness did james yap apply?


The specific component of physical fitness James Yap applied is Agility

Explanation

The body has the ability to work together efficiently aiming for good health while performing tasks in daily life which is called physical fitness.

The specific component of physical fitness that James Yap applied is Agility.

One of the physical abilities needed in sports is agility. For an athlete, agility is needed to help them move around quickly without falling. Agile athletes are athletes who can move quickly and can change the direction of movement in a balanced manner. The better the agility ability in a person, the better the physical processing ability. Thus, agility is one of the basic abilities possessed by athletes.

Basketball agility that allows athletes to return their center of gravity to a vertical position above their base constantly due to the direction of movement that is changed quickly and at an average speed during play. Therefore, the physical fitness component applied by James Yap in dribbling the ball by running fast, changing speed is Agility.

9. ely is three times taller than james and the product of their height is equal to sum of their height increased by 55. find the height of ely and james​


Let

Ely = xJames = y

Equation 1

[tex]x = 3y[/tex]

Equation 2

[tex]xy = x + y + 55[/tex]

Substitute eq. 1 to eq. 2

[tex](3y)y = 3y + y + 55 \\ 3 {y}^{2} = 4y + 55 \\ 3 {y}^{2} - 4y - 55 = 0 \\ (3y + 11)(y - 5) = 0[/tex]

Equate to zero

[tex](3y + 11) \\ 3y + 11 = 0 \\ 3y = - 11 \\ \frac{3y}{3} = \frac{ - 11 }{3} \\ y = - \frac{11}{3} [/tex]

[tex](y - 5) \\ y - 5 = 0 \\ y = 5[/tex]

Substitute y to eq. 1

[tex]y = 5 \\ \\ x = 3y \\ x = 3(5) \\ x =15[/tex]

Therefore, Ely is 15 and James is 5.

13. the average height of peter, paul, and james is 154 cm.if the sum of the heights of peter and paul is 305 cm, how tall is james?


Answer:

157 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

154 x 3 = 462 (Multiply the average height by 3 to get the sum of the heights of peter, paul and james.)

462-305 = 157 (Subtract the heights of peter and paul to 462 to get James' height.)

James is 157 centimeters tall.
