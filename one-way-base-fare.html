21. Narda’s one-way fare to school is ₱ 28.00. If mother gives her ₱ 355.00, about how many days will it last?


Answer:

Narda's ₱ 355.00 allowance will last for approximately 13 days, assuming she spends ₱ 28.00 for her one-way fare to school each day.

Step-by-step explanation:

Number of days = (Total allowance) ÷ (One-way fare)

Number of days = ₱ 355.00 ÷ ₱ 28.00

Number of days = 12.68

Rounding off to the nearest whole number, we get:

Number of days = 13

27. Lina pays 5.50 for one way ride to school for how many two way fares will her 100 be enough?​


Answer:

If Lina pays $5.50 for a one-way ride to school, then for a roundtrip (two-way fare) she would need to pay $5.50 x 2 = $11.

To find how many two-way fares Lina's $100 can afford, we need to divide $100 by $11.

$100 ÷ $11 ≈ 9

So, Lina's $100 will be enough for 9 two-way fares
