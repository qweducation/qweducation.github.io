7. A police station has 25 vehicles of cars and motorcycles. the total number of wheels is 70.find the number of cars and motorcycles the station has


Answer:

There are 10 cars and 15 motorcycles.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the number of cars

Let  y be the number of motorcycles

x + y = 25   -------equation 1

4x + 2y = 70  ------equation 2

From equation 1

x + y = 25

x = 25 - y   ----equation 1'

Substitute equation 1' to equation 2

4x + 2y = 70

4(25 - y) + 2y = 70

100 - 4y + 2y = 70

Transpose

4y - 2y = 100 -70

2y = 30

y = 30/2

y = 15

Substitute y = 15 to equation 1'

x = 25- y

x = 25-15

x = 10

The FPC states that if one event can be done in mm ways and another event can be done in nn ways, the number of ways that the two events can happen is mnmn ways.
