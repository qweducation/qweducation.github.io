PROBLEM:

A passenger plane departed manila at 2:45 p.m. What time did the plane arrive in korea if the flight time from Manila to Korea was 3 hours and 40 minutes?

[tex]\red {\overline{ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: }}[/tex]

ANSWER:

☞ To solve for the time when plane arrived in Korea, we just need to add 3 hours and 40 minutes from the time it departed, 2:45 p.m.

[tex]\begin{gathered} \: \: \: \: \: \: \large \rm 2: 45 \\ \large \rm \: \: \: \: \underline{+ \: \:\: \: 3 : 40 \: \: \: \: \: \: } \\ \large \rm \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: 6: 25 \: pm\end{gathered} [/tex]

Remember that one hour is equivalent to 60 minutes. Thus, 45 + 40 = 85 minutes or 1 hour and 25 minutes.

☞ The plane arrived in Korea at exactly 6:25 p.m.
