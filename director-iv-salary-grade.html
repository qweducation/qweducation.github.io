14. A teacher wants to know if there is a significant difference in the academic performance of senior high school students when grouped according to strand. Thirty students per strand were randomly assigned as respondents of the study. At the end of the semester, their overall grade point average was noted. a. Independent Variable (IV): b. Dependent Variable (DV): 5. A teacher wants to know if there is a significant relationship between salary grade and job satisfaction of government employees. A random sample of 100 government employees with at least one year in service were chosen as respondents of the study. a. Independent Variable (IV): b. Dependent Variable (DV):​


Answer:

1.

a. Independent Variable (IV): strand groupings

b. Dependent Variable (DV): academic performance

The grouping of the students according to strand is the independent variable since this clause exists as it is. Meanwhile, the study will look at the academic performance to see if the grouping affects this, so it is the dependent variable.

2.

a. Independent Variable (IV): salary grade

b. Dependent Variable (DV): job satisfaction

Like in the first question, salary grade already exists as it is so it is the independent variable. On the other hand, job satisfaction is the dependent variable because it is assumed to be different based on the first variable.
