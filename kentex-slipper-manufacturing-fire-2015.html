27. Let's Find Out: Can I Have My Value? Cost C of manufacturing: C = 20x + 15 Cost M of marketing: M = x2 - 3x + 8Income C from sales: R = 2x² + 6x 1. What is the cost of manufacturing 50 pairs of slippers? We will use tge mathematical statement C = 20x + 15.Now, we will substitute 50 to x. We have C = 20(50)+15= 1,015 pesosSo the total cost of manufacturing 50 pairs of slippers is 1,015 pesos. 2. What is the cost of marketing 50 pairs of slippers? 3. What is the total cost of manufacturing and marketing 50 pairs of slippers? 4. What is the income from selling 50 pairs of slippers? 5. What is the cost of manufacturing 20 pairs of slippers? ​


Answer:

since number 1 seems to be an explanation/lesson, I will use it as guide.

2. We will use the mathematical equation of Cost of Marketing = x² - 3x + 8

Now, we will substitute 50 to x

We have M = (50)² - 3(50) + 8

So the total cost of marketing 50 pairs of slippers is Php2358.00

3. We will use the mathemitical equation for marketing and manufacturing which are C = 20x + 15 and M= x² - 3x + 8

Substitute 50 to x for each equation

C = 20(50) + 15

= 1015

M = (50)² - 3x + 8

= 2385

Now, we have to add them. 1015 + 2358 = 3373

So the total cost of manufacturing and marketing 50 pairs of slippers is Php3373.00

4. We will use the mathematical equation for Income, R = 2x² + 6x

Substitute 50 to x

R = 2(50)² + 6(50)

= 5300

So the total income from selling 50 pairs of slippers is Php5300.00

5. We will use the mathematical equation for manufacturing, C=20x + 15

Substitute 20 to x

C = 20(20) + 15

= 415

So the total cost of manufacturing 20 pairs of slippers is Php415.00
