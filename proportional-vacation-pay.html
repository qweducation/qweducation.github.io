1. the cost of a vacation to siargao island is 4,500 and you are required to pay 1/5 of that when you reserve the trip how much will you pay? ​


Answer:

You have to pay  20% of Php 4,500.00 or Php 900.00.

Step-by-step explanation:

First: Analyze the problem.

The cost of a vacation to Siargao island is 4,500 and you are required to pay 1/5 of that when you reserve the trip how much will you pay? ​

The cost is Php 4,500.00

Reservation fee is 1/5 of the cost.

How much will you pay?

Second: Transform to mathematical equation.

Convert the fraction to decimal so that we can find the percent.

[tex]\frac{1}{5} =0.20[/tex]

Now, we multiply it to find the percentage, whose equation is [tex]P=RB[/tex] wherein;

[tex]P[/tex] is the amount we have to pay,

[tex]R[/tex] is the rate of the reservation fee, 0.20.

[tex]B[/tex] is the base or the total cost of the payment, Php 4,500.

[tex]B=(0.20)(4,500)[/tex]

[tex]B=900[/tex]
