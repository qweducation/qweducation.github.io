3. Who is lafcadio hearn(the story of yongle bell)..must enough info


Answer:

Story of the Great Bell

Explanation:

The story of the great bell is written by Lafcadio Hearn. The story is all about Ko-Ngai's father, Kouan Yu, was asked by the King to make the great bell. Together with the other workers, they strive hard to make the bell. Unfortunately, the bell was not successfully made after their first and second try. Kouan Yu told the king about it. The Son of Heaven became angry and  sent a letter wrapped in a royal yellow, sealed with a dragon telling that if they cannot make the bell, they shall be beheaded. After some time, Ko-Ngai knew about the letter sent by the king to her father. He went to an astrologer to seek for a solution. The astrologer said that a blood and flesh of a maiden must be offered to make the great bell successfully. The day comes when Ko-Ngai made a decision to leap into the furnace leaving one of her shoe on the ground. Ko-Ngai's blood and flesh made the great bell durable and strong which can be heard a hundred li. The ring of the bell was reminded of Ko-Ngai crying to her shoe.
