Answer:

I can suggest some ways on how to conserve and promote the efficient use of electricity:

Use energy-efficient appliances and devices. Opt for appliances that have the Energy Star label, as they are designed to use less energy while still delivering the same performance.

Turn off the lights and unplug appliances when not in use. Even when not in use, appliances and devices can still consume electricity when they are plugged in.

Use natural light whenever possible. Open curtains and blinds during the day to allow natural light in, and only turn on lights when necessary.

Set air conditioning units to an appropriate temperature. Keeping your air conditioning unit at a moderate temperature can help reduce energy consumption.

Use ceiling fans. Ceiling fans use less energy compared to air conditioning units, and they can help circulate cool air during hot weather.

Use power strips. Power strips can help conserve electricity by allowing you to turn off multiple appliances and devices with just one switch.

Invest in renewable energy sources. Consider installing solar panels or wind turbines to generate your own electricity.

Educate yourself and others on energy conservation. Encourage your family and friends to conserve energy by sharing your knowledge and experiences.

Participate in energy-saving programs. Check with your local electric company for energy-saving programs that can help you reduce your energy consumption.

Lastly, support policies and initiatives that promote the use of renewable energy sources and the reduction of carbon emissions. Every little effort counts in saving the planet.

26. 1.) A woman plans to travel to baguio. She checks theWeather update and the average temperature for nextweek will be 17 degree celcius. what is it in Farenheit?in kelvin ? in Rankine?​


Answer:

62.6 ℉, 290.15 K, 522.27 °R

Explanation:

Fahrenheit

(17°C × 9/5) + 32 = 62.6 °F

Kelvin

17°C + 273.15 = 290.15 K

Rankine

17°C × 9/5 + 491.67 = 522.27 °R
