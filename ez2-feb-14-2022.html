Answer:

a. To determine the resale value of the iPhone 14 in 2025, we need to calculate the depreciated value of the phone after three years. The phone depreciates by 20% per year, so after three years, the value of the phone is:

52,000 x 0.8 x 0.8 x 0.8 = Php 26,624

Therefore, the resale value of the iPhone 14 in 2025 is Php 26,624.

b. A function that models the value of the iPhone 14 can be expressed as:

V(t) = 52,000(0.8)^t

where V(t) is the value of the phone at time t (measured in years since 2022).
