28. The time in Manila is 6 hours ahead of Rome.The flight from Manila to Rome takes 9 hours.If an airplane leaves manila by 9:00a.m.at what time in Rome will the plane arrive


Answer:

The plane will arrive in Rome 12 noon or 12:00 p.m.

Step-by-step explanation:

Manila is 6 hours ahead of Rome:

Manila time: 9:00  or 9 a.m.

Rome time: 3:00  or 3 a.m.

Flying time from  Manila to Rome: 9 hours

Departure Manila: 9:00

Arrival in Rome, Manila time: 9:00 + 9 = 18:00 or 6 pm.

Rome time: 18:00 - 6 = 12:00

The plane will arrive in Rome 12 noon or 12:00 p.m
