11. Abide by Labor Laws – These includes laws related to how much an employee can be paid, how many hours he can work, and the criteria which he can be hired and fired. explanation about Abide by Labor laws? ​


Answer:

Abiding by labor laws means following and complying with the legal regulations and requirements that are imposed on employers and employees in the workplace. These laws aim to protect employees' rights and ensure that they are treated fairly and equitably in terms of pay, work hours, and employment conditions. Abiding by labor laws is essential for maintaining a positive work environment, minimizing labor disputes and legal actions, and promoting the welfare of employees. Failure to comply with these laws can result in legal fines and penalties, damage to the company's reputation, and loss of employee trust and loyalty. Overall, abiding by labor laws is a necessary and significant aspect of responsible and ethical business management.
