18. Subscripts refer to the atom directly before it (H2O) . Why shouldn't subscripts be changed? ​


#BrainlyFast

Subscript refers to the number of elements present in the composition of a certain compound or molecule. For the substance H₂O, it represent that there are two hydrogen atom in a single molecule of H₂O. When we try to balance a chemical reaction, we can only put a coefficient before the compound or substance. We don't have to change the subscript since it will change the chemical composition of a substance.

We need to balance the equation in every  chemical reaction since according to the law of conservation of mass, "during chemical reaction, atoms are not destroyed or change" which means, the mass of reactant will always be equal to the mass of the product.

In balancing, we can add the coefficient which is the number before the element or the compound in the equation. coefficient also must be a whole number not a fraction or decimal number.

25. subscription received in the year 2004 is 17500which include 400 2003 and and 600 for 2005at the end of 2004 subscription outstanding at the end of the year 500 the subscription due but not received at the end of the year that is 2003 where rupees 600 . what amount should be credited in income and expenditure account and subscription ?


Answer:

h

Step-by-step explanation:

Subscription received in 2003 = 400

Subscription received in 2004 = 17,500 - 400 - 600 = 16,500

Subscription received in 2005 = 600

Total subscription received = 400 + 16,500 + 600 = 17,500

Subscription outstanding at the end of 2004 = 500

Subscription due but not received at the end of 2003 = 600

Now, we can calculate the subscription earned in 2004 as follows:

Subscription earned in 2004 = Total subscription received - Subscription outstanding at the end of 2004 + Subscription due but not received at the end of 2003

Subscription earned in 2004 = 17,500 - 500 + 600

Subscription earned in 2004 = 17,600
