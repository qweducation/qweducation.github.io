16. Read The Story SEVENTH GRADE by Gary Soto and summarize it.


Answer:

The story is taken in a middle school. The conflict is that Victor, the protagonist, is trying to impress a girl named Teresa, but he doesn’t know how to. He was in homeroom and saw that Teresa was there too. After all his classes and lunch, he goes to French class, which Teresa is there as well. At the same time, he wanted to impress Teresa. As a result of this, he pretended to speak French to the teacher and he felt really embarrassed for doing that. After class, Teresa was actually impressed and asked Victor for help, so Victor, feeling determined, went to the library after his classes and took some French books so he can help Teresa. Victor goes through events that he has never faced before. He is a dynamic character because he grows from being awkward to daring to determined.
