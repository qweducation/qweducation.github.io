6. Marian invested Php 150,000.00 last April 28, 2021 at 8% exact interest. Using approximate time, find the interest and final amount of the investment if she will withdraw her money by February 12, 2022.


The interest on the investment if she will withdraw her money by February 12, 2022 is Php11,000 and the final amount is Php161,000.

From this problem, is defined below:

Given that,

Amount of money invested on April 28, 2021 = Php150,000

Rate of interest = 8 %

The interest amount and final are to be paid on February 28, 2022.

Interest is accrued on February 12, 2022.

Time period = From April 28, 2021, to February 12, 2022

                   = 11 months

Accrued Interest amount = Principal amount × Interest rate × Time period

                                         = Php150,000 × 0.08 × (11 ÷ 12)

                                         = Php11,000

The final amount = Php150,000 + Php11,000

                            = Php161,000

Therefore, the adjusting journal entry is as follows:

Interest receivable A/c Dr.   Php11,000

        To interest income                          Php11,000

(To record the interest on the amount given to employees is accrued and adjusted)
