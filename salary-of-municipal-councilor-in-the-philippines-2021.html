1. sino ang municipal councilors ​


Ang mga municipal councilors ay ang mga halal na kinatawan ng bawat bayan o munisipalidad sa Pilipinas. Sila ay bahagi ng Sangguniang Bayan ng bawat bayan at nagtatrabaho upang magpasa ng mga lokal na ordinansa at mga resolusyon na may kaugnayan sa pamamahala ng bayan. Ang mga konsehal ay kinakailangan na maging residente ng bayan at may edad na hindi bababa sa 18 taong gulang. Sila ay nahahalal sa loob ng tatlong taon at maaari ring maglingkod ng hanggang tatlong sunod na termino. Ang bilang ng mga konsehal sa bawat bayan ay nakabatay sa populasyon ng bayan.

3. what is municipal councilors responsibility and duties​


Council roles and responsibilitiesThe council is the governing body of the municipal corporation and the custodian of its powers, both legislative and administrativeThe Municipal Government Act (MGA) provides that councils can only exercise the powers of the municipal corporation in the proper form, either by bylaw or resolution.

Councillor Duties

consider the welfare and interests of the municipality as a whole and, to bring to council's attention anything that would promote the welfare or interests of the municipalityparticipate generally in developing and evaluating the policies and programs of the municipalityparticipate in council meetings and council committee meetings and meetings of other bodies they are appointed to by the councilobtain information about the operation or administration of the municipality from the chief administrative officerkeep in confidence matters discussed in private at a council meeting until discussed at a meeting held in publicperform any other duty or function imposed on councillors by this or any other enactment or by the council.
