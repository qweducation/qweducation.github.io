17. Who shall take over when there is a vacancy in the Office of the Vice President? What rules apply?


Explanation:

Whenever there is a vacancy in the Office of the Vice-President during the term for which he was elected, the President shall nominate a Vice-President from among the Members of the Senate and the House of Representatives who shall assume office upon confirmation by a majority vote of all the Members of both Houses
