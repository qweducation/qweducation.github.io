14. the game of volleyball was invented​


Answer:

Volleyball was invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan, physical director of the Young Men's Christian Association (YMCA) in Holyoke, Massachusetts. It was designed as an indoor sport for businessmen who found the new game of basketball too vigorous.

MANILA, Philippines – Thailand continued its dominant stretch in the 2019 Southeast Asian Games but not before enduring a late scare against a feisty Philippines side, 25-16, 25-22, 34-32, in women’s volleyball action at the PhilSports Arena on Thursday, December 5.
