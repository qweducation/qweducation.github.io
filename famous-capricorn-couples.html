8. What is tropic lf capricorn


The tropic of capricorn is an imaginary line that is found in the southern hemisphere. If you will look at the map, you can see that it is located at around 23º 26' at the south of equator. It is one of the five major circles of latitude found on Earth. It was named as capricorn because at around 2000 years ago the sun used to enter the constellation Capricornus during the winter solstice.

10. kabuluhan ng tropic of cancer at capricorn​


Answer:

The Tropic of Capricorn or Southern tropic is one of the five major circles of latitude that mark maps of the Earth. It is the parallel of latitude at 23 degrees 26′ 22″ south of the Equator, and is the farthest southern latitude that the sun can appear directly overhead, occurring on the December solstice.

The Tropic of Cancer, which is also referred to as the Northern Tropic, is the most northerly circle of latitude on Earth at which the Sun can be directly overhead. This occurs on the June solstice, when the Northern Hemisphere is tilted toward the Sun to its maximum extent.

14. what is the meaning of tropic of capricorn


ANSWER:

The Tropic of Capricorn is the circle of latitude that contains the subsolar point at the December solstice. It is thus the southernmost latitude where the Sun can be seen directly overhead. It also reaches 90 degrees below the horizon at solar midnight on the June Solstice.
