5. According to the Nicaraguan poet Daisy Zamora, poetry is:


For the Nicaraguan poet Daisy Zamora, poetry is a platform for activism. Her poems reflect life and its struggles especially the fight for women's rights. Her work is known for her strong stance on human rights, politics, feminism, etc. She was also an active part of the resistance against the Somoza dictatorship in 1973. She was even exiled to Hondurias, Panama and Costa Rica. Her literary work is a household in the Latin American literature as it resonates the struggles of the society. She authored 5 widely-read books in Spanish. Through poetry, she has given voice to those who are powerless and abused.

28. Angela invited 300 kids to her birthday today. Only 15% of the kids did not show up. How many kids came to the party?(SHOW YOUR SOLUTION) ​


Angela invited 300 kids to her birthday today. Only 15% of the kids did not show up. How many kids came to the party?

[tex]\sf{No.\:of\:children\:invited\:to\:the\:party=300}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{Percentage\:of\:students\:who\:didn't\:show\:up=15\%}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{So,\:percentage\:of\:children\:who\:showed\:up=100-15=85\%}[/tex]

No. of children who showed up = 85% of 300

[tex]\sf{=\dfrac{85}{100}\times300}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{=85\times3}[/tex]

[tex]\sf{=255}[/tex]

255 kids showed up on Angela's birthday party.
