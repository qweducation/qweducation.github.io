19. IV. Give the correct meaning of the following acronyms and write a short description about their role/duties. (10 points) 1. COA: 2. DILG: 3. DSWD 4. DOLE: 5. DOJ:​


Answer:

1. COA stands for Commission on Audit. Its role is to ensure transparency and accountability in government transactions. The COA conducts audits and examinations of the accounts and records of government agencies and instrumentalities to ensure that funds are properly accounted for and used for their intended purposes.

2. DILG stands for Department of the Interior and Local Government. Its role is to oversee and coordinate local governance and ensure public safety. The DILG is responsible for the administration and supervision of local government units, as well as the maintenance of peace and order.

3. DSWD stands for Department of Social Welfare and Development. Its role is to promote the social welfare and development of Filipinos, particularly the poor, vulnerable, and marginalized. The DSWD provides social protection programs and services, such as cash assistance, livelihood support, and protection services for children, women, and persons with disabilities.

4. DOLE stands for Department of Labor and Employment. Its role is to promote gainful employment opportunities and protect workers' welfare and rights. The DOLE oversees the labor market and implements labor laws, policies, and programs to ensure that workers are treated fairly and equitably.

5. DOJ stands for Department of Justice. Its role is to administer justice and uphold the rule of law. The DOJ is responsible for the prosecution of criminal offenses, the provision of legal advice to government agencies, and the protection of the rights and interests of the state and the people.
