5. is it possible to use accounting in your daily activities?How ? ​


Answer:

Yes, it is possible to use accounting in your daily activities. Here are some ways you can incorporate accounting principles into your daily life:

- Budgeting: You can create a budget to track your income and expenses, and ensure that you are not overspending.

- Tracking Expenses: You can keep track of your daily expenses by recording them in a spreadsheet or using an app, which can help you identify areas where you can cut back on spending.

- Bill Payments: You can keep track of your bill payments, including due dates and amounts, and ensure that they are paid on time to avoid late fees and penalties.

- Saving: You can set aside a portion of your income for savings and invest it in a savings account or other investment options.

- Tax Planning: You can plan your taxes by keeping track of deductible expenses, such as charitable contributions, business expenses, and home office expenses.

- Financial Reporting: You can create financial reports, such as a personal balance sheet or income statement, to help you understand your overall financial situation and make informed decisions.

By using accounting principles in your daily life, you can gain a better understanding of your finances and make more informed financial decisions.

20. How to know if bpi account is still active?


To check if your BPI account is still active, just try to access it at any BPI ATM or call their 24-hour hotline, 89-100.

Only active accounts can be viewed on BPI expressonline. If the status of your account becomes inactive or closed, your account will not be displayed anymore. The system automatically removes online access to these accounts because of their status.

If you want to know other BPI account if it is active, you can go to any BPI branch over-the-counter, then try to ask the teller (make a reason, like you want to deposit to the account). She can then check if the account you're inquiring is still active.

28. Define what is accounting? How accounting used in our daily activities? ​


Answer:

Accounting is the process of recording financial transactions pertaining to a business. The accounting process includes summarizing, analyzing, and reporting these transactions to oversight agencies, regulators, and tax collection entities. We use accounting to check our finances, manage our bills, and even for something as simple as buying groceries. Also, accounting plays a vital role in running a business because it helps you track income and expenditures, ensure statutory compliance, and provide investors, management, and government with quantitative financial information which can be used in making business decisions.
