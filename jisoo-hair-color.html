Suppose the density of an object increases and the mass of each volume will also increase. The average density of an object is obtained from the total mass divided by the total volume.Density is a theory that helps in determining the types of substances that will float and will sink when placed in a liquid. Usually, the type of substance that will float is a substance that has a density less than the density of the liquid.

20. 31. In how many ways can Lalisa, Rose', Jennie, and Jisoo be seated at a round table? a. 1 b. 2 C.6 d. 24​


Answer:

There are 6 ways to seat Lalisa, Rose', Jennie, and Jisoo at a round table.

To see why, we can list out all the possible arrangements. However, since the table is round, some of the arrangements will be considered the same if we rotate the table. To account for this, we can fix one person in place and arrange the other three around them.

For example, we can fix Lalisa in place and arrange the other three around her. There are 3 choices for the person to her immediate right, 2 choices for the person to her immediate left, and 1 choice for the remaining person. This gives us:

3 x 2 x 1 = 6

However, we can also fix any of the other three people in place and arrange the remaining three around them. This gives us a total of:

6 x 4 = 24

possible arrangements. However, since the table is round, each arrangement can be rotated, giving us the same arrangement in a different orientation. Therefore, we need to divide by the number of people to account for the rotations:

24 / 4 = 6

Therefore, the answer is c. 6.
