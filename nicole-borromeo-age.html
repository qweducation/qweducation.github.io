23. Kevin is four years younger than Nicole. In 7 years, Kevin will be 4/5 the age of Nicole. How old is Kevin?(Write your answer in this format: ___ years old) ​


AGE PROBLEMSProblem:

Kevin is four years younger than Nicole. In 7 years, Kevin will be 4/5 the age of Nicole. How old is Kevin?

Solution:

With the given data, we can first list them down and analyze in order to solve for the required data. Let us put the present and future.

• Present

Let Kevin's age be "x" and Nicole's age be "x+4".

• Future (After 7 years)

Kevin's age will be "x+7" and Nicole's age be "x+4+7" which can be simplified to "x+11".

Using this, we can formula an equation.

Since Kevin's age is 4/5 of Nicole's age 7 years later, then the equation will be: K = 4/5N, where K is Kevin's age and N is Nicole's age in this equation.

But to recall, 7 year later, Kevin's age is also x+7 while Nicole's age is x+11. With this, we will substitute it to the derived equation above, so:

K = 4/5N

x+7 = 4/5(x+11)

Let us multiply both side by 5 to get:

5(x+7) = 4(x+11)

And distribute each to simplify:

5x + 35 = 4x + 44

Transpose 4x to the left side of the equation and 35 to the right side:

5x - 4x = 44 - 35

x = 9

Remember that x is equal to Kevin's age.

To check if our answer is correct:

• Present

Kevin = x = 9

Nicole = x+4 = 13

• Future

Kevin = x+7 = 9+7 = 16

Nicole = x+11 = 9+11 = 20

16/20 = 4/5

So 16 is 4/5 of 20.

Therefore, Kevin's age is 9 years old.

PROBLEM:

Currently, Andy is three times Nicol's age. In ten years, Andy will be twelve years older than Nicol. What are their ages now?

[tex]\red {\overline{ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: }} [/tex]

SOLUTION:Current Age

☞ Let the current age be our independent variable. Thus, Nicol's age be x and Andy's 3x since it's three times the age of Nicol.

Nicol - xAndy - 3xIn Ten Years

☞ Since it's after ten years, we'll add 10 from their current ages, thus, for Nicol be x + 10 while for Andy, 3x + 10.

☞ But, there's another condition, Andy's age is 12 more than that of Nicol. If Nicol is x + 10 years old, then Andy will be x + 10 + 12 years old.

Nicol - x + 10Andy - x + 10 + 12 or 3x + 10

☞ To get their ages, let's find first the value of x.

[tex] \large \rm 3x + 10 = x + 10 + 12[/tex]

[tex] \large \rm 3x + 10 = x + 22[/tex]

[tex] \large \rm 3x - x = 22 - 10[/tex]

[tex] \large \rm 2x = 12[/tex]

[tex] \large \rm x = 6[/tex]

[tex]\red {\overline{ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: }} [/tex]

ANSWER:Now, Nicol's age is six while Andy is [tex]\rm 3x=3(6)=18[/tex] years old.

Nicol's age is six while Andy is 18 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Currently, Andrei is three times Nicolas' age. In ten years, Andrei will be twelve years older than Nicolas. What are their ages now?

Andrei's age in defined in terms of Nicolos' age, so I'll pick a variable for Nicolas' age now; say, "N". This allows me to create an expression for Andrei's age now, which is three times that of Nicolas.

Nicolas' age now: N

Andrei's age now: 3N

In ten years, they each will be ten years older, so I'll add 10 to each of the above for their later ages.

Nicolas' age later: N + 10

Andrei's age later: 3N + 10

But I am also given that, in ten years, Andrei will be twelve years older than Nicolas. So I can create another expression for Andrei's age in ten years; namely, I'll take the expression for Nicolas' age in ten years, and add twelve to that.

Andrei's age later: [N + 10] + 12

Since Andrei's future age will equal his future age, I can take these two expressions for his future age, set them equal (creating an equation), and solve for the value of the variable.

3N + 10 = [N + 10] + 12

3N + 10 = N + 22

2N + 10 = 22

2N = 12

N = 6
