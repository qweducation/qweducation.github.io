28. how can the department of trade and industry(DTI) help the customers?​


Answer:

The Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) plays a vital role in helping customers in a variety of ways. One of its primary functions is to ensure that consumers are protected from unfair business practices and that their rights are upheld. The DTI does this by enforcing consumer protection laws and regulations, investigating complaints from consumers, and imposing penalties on companies that violate these laws. Additionally, the DTI provides resources and information to consumers to help them make informed decisions when purchasing goods and services. These resources include information on product safety, consumer rights, and tips on how to avoid scams and fraudulent activities. The DTI also works to promote fair competition among businesses, which benefits consumers by providing them with more choices, better quality products, and lower prices. Overall, the DTI plays a crucial role in safeguarding consumer interests and helping them make informed decisions when interacting with businesses.
