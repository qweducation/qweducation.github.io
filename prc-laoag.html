10. wind speed of typhoon yolanda in laoag


Typhoon Haiyan (locally known as Bagyong Yolanda) is the super typhoon and the most powerful typhoon the Philippines has ever experienced. The typhoon had a landfall at Guiuan, Eastern Samar last November 8, 2013. A lot of people have died and a lot properties have been destroyed by this natural disaster. Its wind speed reached 300 kilometer per hour (kph). Signal numbers have been announced on provinces that will be affected by the typhoon as a warning. The city of Laoag in Ilocos Norte was never included among the affected areas of the Typhoon Yolanda since the path of the typhoon was constantly moving northwest with only a slight increase in altitude. The estimated wind speed in Laoag City during the occurrence of Yolanda was just below 30 kph (since the lower end of Signal Number One is 30 kph). Unlike the affected places like Tacloban experienced a wind speed of at least 315 kph as they are classified under Signal Number 4, the whole province of Cebu experienced an average wind speed ranging from 61 to 185 kph (depending if its Southern Cebu that is under Signal Number 2, Metro Cebu that is under Signal Number 3, and Northern Cebu that is under Signal number 4).

15. Ano ang kahulugan ng PRC?


Ano ang Kahulugan ng PRC?

Ang kahulugan ng PRC ay Philippine Regulation Commission. Ito ay isang kagawaran ng gobyerno. Ito ang may hawak at nagbibigay ng mga pagsusulit para sa mga propesyonal ng bansa. Ang pagsusulit na ito ay tinatawag na board exam. Sila rin ang magiisyu ng mga lisensya nila pagkatapos nila matapos ang kanilang mga board exam.

Kung ibabase sa kahulugan ng PRC, mapapansin mo na agad dito ang ginagampanang tungkulin ng ahensyang ito:

magpatupad ng mga batas na siyang dapat sundin ng bawat propesyon at trabaho sa bansa

Kung ibabase sa batas, ang PRC ay mayroong dalawang tungkulin na ginagampanan sa ating bansa:

Mag-patupad ng mga pagsusulit o board exam para sa lisensya ng mga nais maging isang propesyonalMangasiwa sa mga propesyonal ng ating bansa sa kanilang paggampan sa kanilang kaniya kaniyang propesyon.

21. Ano ang prc at ano ang tungkulin nito


Ang PRC o ang Professional Regulation Commission ay ang ahensiyang nangagasiwa sa pagsasanay ng propesyonal (maliban sa mga abugado- sila ay pinangangasiwaan ng Kataastaasang Hukuman (Supreme Court).

Ang PRC o Professional Regulation Commission ay bahagi ng Department of Labor and Employment. Ang gawain nito ay ang pag -aayos at pangasiwaan ang pagsasanay ng mga propesyonal na bumubuo sa highly skilled manpower ng bansa. Ang PRC ay may isang mahalagang papel sa pagbuo ng sa mga propesyonal para makatulong sa industriya, komersiyo, pamumuno, at ekonomiya.

28. ano ang tungkulin ng prc


The Professional Regulation Commission, (Filipino: Komisyon sa Regulasyon ng mga Propesyon) otherwise known as the PRC, is a three-man commission attached to Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE). Its mandate is to regulate and supervise the practice of the professionals (except lawyers, who are handled by the Supreme Court of the Philippines) who constitute the highly skilled manpower of the country. As the agency-in-charge of the professional sector, the PRC plays a strategic role in developing the corps of professionals for industry, commerce, governance, and the economy.
