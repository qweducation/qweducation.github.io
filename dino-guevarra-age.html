Given Data

Let say : age = y

age of Dino = y

age of Dina = 2y  since she have twice the age of Dino

Solution:

Dina (age) + Dino (age) = 84 years

                                   y   = 84 years / 3

      y = 2x = Dina's age

Required: Dina's age (y)

Solution:

x + y = 84 (equation 1)

substitute y=2x to equation 1

x + 2x = 84

solve for x

3x = 84

divide both sides by 3

x = 28

solve for dina's age

y = 2x = 2(28) = 56 years old
