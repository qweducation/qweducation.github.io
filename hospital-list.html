29. Identify one (1) chemical you can find in each place listed below. Church Hospital Environment Market Industry School Transportation Restaurant Your bedroom


Examples of chemicals that we can find around us.

Chemicals are pure substances or mixtures with various chemical elements.

In everyday life, we will always be in contact with chemicals.

Here are some examples of chemicals that can be found all around us:

1. In church.

Because the church is a place of worship, maybe we will not find many objects that are chemicals. But during the pandemic, we can easily find hand sanitizer in many places, especially churches, which are places where many people gather during worship. Hand sanitizer contains alcohol which is an example of a chemical substance.

2. In the hospital

Drugs are the most common examples of chemicals that we can find in hospitals. Each medicine contains different chemicals that are used for healing.

3. In Environment

The oxygen we breathe is the easiest chemical we can find. There is also carbon dioxide for photosynthetic plants and also water which is the main human need. These three things are examples of chemicals.

4. In the market

Baking powder, a food ingredient that is usually used to make cakes and sold on the market is one example of a chemical containing sodium bicarbonate (NaHCO₃).

5. In the industry

Boric acid is one of the chemicals in the industrial field. These chemicals can be used to make cement, preservatives, and to process wood.

6. At school

In school, we can find many chemicals in science laboratories. Examples are hydrochloric acid (HCl) and methanol (CH3OH).

7. On transportation

Gasoline, the fuel used by motorized vehicles is one example of a chemical substance in transportation. Gasoline contains many different chemicals, examples of which are alkanes and alkenes.

8. At the restaurant

Hand soap, which is usually available in restaurant toilets, is an example of a chemical. Hand washing soap contains sodium compounds.

9. In the bedroom

Chemicals that can be found in the bedroom are skincare that we usually use and store in the room. Skincare contains a variety of chemicals that function to treat the skin.
