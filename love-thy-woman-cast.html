Answer:

The tree is 25 feet

Step-by-step explanation:

The woman was 5/6th of the shadow so if you divide the length of the shadow to 6 and multiply it by 5 to get the 5/6th size of the tree then the equation would be:

30ft ÷ 6 = 5 × 5 = 25ft
