Answer:

3

Step-by-step explanation:

50×0.40=20

20×0.85=17

20-17=3

Answer:There are 3 students who passed the scholarship exam did not belong to the honor list.

Explanation:First, find how many students passed.

[tex]50*\frac{40}{100} =\frac{2000}{100} =20[/tex]

Then, find how many passers belong to honor list.

[tex]20*\frac{85}{100} =\frac{1700}{100} =17[/tex]

Last, subtract the honor students from students passed the exam.

[tex]20-17=3[/tex]

Even the Romans, not known for their human rights, abolished crucifixion in AD 337 considering it too inhumane.
